## AI Use

Solo-tuned encounter tables for the Ashgarden Vale, by sub-zone. **Designed for ONE player character.** The Vale is the gentlest Ring 1 region — most results are social or tragic, not combat; the lethal exception is Marrow Cross. Roll d12 by sub-zone or pick to fit the scene/clocks. Stat references are D&D 5e/2024-compatible baselines (no copied stat blocks); scale per Scaling Notes. Always telegraph danger; always offer a non-combat option.

## Encounter Type Legend
- **[Dead]** = a Remembrance/haunting — usually resolvable by a true rite (Religion DC 13–15) or parley (Insight/Persuasion DC 12).
- **[Folk]** = NPCs (pilgrims, farmers, Ledger men, scholars, drovers); mostly social.
- **[Hazard]** = environmental; a check, not a fight.
- **[Clue]** = a breadcrumb toward a mystery (deliver, don't over-explain; cap Vale reveals at R1 in Act 1).

## Scaling Notes

- **Lvl 1–2:** drop wrathful-dead results to single, weakened Remembrances; emphasize social/clue results; Marrow Cross is a place to *avoid*, telegraphed.
- **Lvl 3–4 (center of mass):** as written.
- **Lvl 5–6:** wrathful dead come in pairs; Ledger carts gain a Veteran-like guard; Remnant presence at the ruins sharpens into rivalry; add thin-touch relics at dig-sites.
- **Always:** Remembrances default non-hostile and layable; combat is the player's choice, never the table's demand. The Vale's signature is *the tragedy of denial*, not slaughter.
