# _PLACEHOLDER.md — /13_encounters_and_bestiary/

## Purpose

This folder will contain encounter tables, adversary group profiles, custom monster entries, recurring villain profiles, boss fight designs, and noncombat obstacle descriptions.

## Current Status

**Empty — Stage 0 scaffold.** Encounter and bestiary content will be created during Stage 3 (regional encounter tables) and Stage 13 (full expansion).

## Expected File Types

- `ENCOUNTER_TABLES.md` — regional random encounter tables
- `ADVERSARY_GROUPS.md` — faction-aligned enemy groups
- `RECURRING_VILLAINS.md` — named enemy profiles
- `BOSS_DESIGN.md` — boss encounter notes and solo-safe design
- `NONCOMBAT_OBSTACLES.md` — hazards, puzzles, and social encounters
- `MONSTER_INDEX.md` — custom or modified monsters used in the campaign
- Individual encounter files for significant set-piece battles

## Solo-Play Design Requirement

All encounter content must account for a solo character. See `01_runner_protocol/COMBAT_PROTOCOL.md` and `01_runner_protocol/SOLO_PLAY_PRINCIPLES.md`.
