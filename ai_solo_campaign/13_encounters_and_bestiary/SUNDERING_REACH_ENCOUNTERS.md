## AI Use

Solo-tuned encounter tables for the Sundering Reach, by zone. **Designed for ONE player character** — most "encounters" are non-combat (a haunting to resolve, a traveler to meet, a hazard to navigate), and combats use single foes or small, telegraphed groups with morale and escape routes. Roll d12 by zone, or pick to fit the scene/clocks. Stat references use D&D 5e/2024-compatible baselines (no copied stat blocks); scale to PC level per Scaling Notes. Always telegraph danger; always offer a non-combat option (`COMBAT_PROTOCOL.md`, `SOLO_PLAY_PRINCIPLES.md`).

## Encounter Type Legend
- **[Dead]** = a Remembrance/haunting — usually resolvable by a true rite (Religion DC 13) or parley (Insight/Persuasion DC 12–13); combat optional.
- **[Beast]** = mundane fen/highland predator; telegraphed; flees on morale.
- **[Folk]** = NPCs (travelers, faction agents, bandits, smugglers); mostly social.
- **[Hazard]** = environmental; a check, not a fight.
- **[Clue]** = a breadcrumb toward a mystery (deliver, don't over-explain).

## Solo-Play Encounter Rules (apply to all zones)

- **Default to non-combat:** [Dead] and [Folk] results resolve by rite, parley, or social play unless the player chooses or forces a fight.
- **Single foes or small groups:** never field a "party-sized" mob against a solo PC without allies, terrain, stealth, or escape routes.
- **Telegraph + morale:** describe danger first; foes flee/parley on morale (typically at half HP or when their goal fails).
- **Retreat always exists:** every zone has a marked way back to a settlement or the Ashwalk Rest.
- **Allies:** Old Sashe (fens), Brother Hale/Wardens (dead), the watch (towns) can be present to ease danger.

## Scaling Notes
- **Lvl 1–2:** prefer rows that are [Folk]/[Hazard]/[Clue] or a single weak [Dead]; soften DCs by 1–2.
- **Lvl 3–4:** standard as written.
- **Lvl 5–6:** upgrade single Remembrances to wraith-like (~36–45 HP), add a second foe, raise hazard DCs by 2; the Heights and Greyfens-deep become the focus.
- Recurring named foes (Veska Dunn, Cole Ashby, Tallytooth Ren) scale with their NPC entries; never let them die cheaply (they're recurring rivals).
