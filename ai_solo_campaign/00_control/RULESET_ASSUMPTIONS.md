# RULESET_ASSUMPTIONS.md

Primary rules target: D&D 5e / 2024-compatible.

Default assumptions:
- Use 2024-style terminology where practical.
- Keep mechanics compatible enough that a 5e DM can adapt them.
- Do not invent a replacement ruleset.
- Do not require proprietary setting lore.
- Do not copy official stat blocks verbatim.
- Use abbreviated D&D-compatible stat profiles when full stat blocks are unnecessary.

When uncertain, favor simple 5e-compatible mechanics over elaborate homebrew.
