# GENERATION_GUARDRAILS.md

## Do Not Drift Into

- Generic fantasy wiki
- Rules-light storygame
- Pure novel outline
- Video game quest log
- Random table soup
- Unindexed lore dump
- Mechanics-free setting
- Four-PC party adventure path
- Fully improvised world
- Joke campaign
- Grimdark-only misery campaign
- Chosen-one railroad

## Always Preserve

- Predetermined-first, improvisation-second
- D&D 5e / 2024-compatible mechanics
- Solo-play usability
- Hidden DM-only truth
- Open-world agency
- Faction consequences
- Mystery solvability
- Cross-linked authored content
- State tracking
- Index updates

## Before Creating New Major Content

Check:
- Does this contradict canon?
- Does something like this already exist?
- Is this indexed?
- Does it have play function?
- Does it connect to NPCs, factions, quests, clues, or locations?
- Does it need D&D mechanics?
- Is it player-safe, DM-only, or mixed?
