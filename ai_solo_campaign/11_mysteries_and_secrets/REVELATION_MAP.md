## Purpose

**DM-ONLY.** Maps the sequence of revelations across the campaign — what the player can discover, when, through which paths, and what changes when each revelation lands. The AI DM uses this to control pacing of secrets and to avoid revealing the campaign's central truth too early.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — Not yet populated.** To be established during Stage 1.

## AI Use

Consult before delivering a major clue or revelation. Check whether this revelation is appropriate for the current act. Check what it will unblock or foreclose. Do not quote to player.
