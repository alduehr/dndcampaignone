# REVEAL_TIMING.md

## AI Use

Quick-reference chart of phase gates for all major revelations. Use this to confirm whether the campaign has reached the minimum phase before delivering a revelation. Full details (clue sources, prereqs, consequences) are in `REVELATION_MAP.md` (DM-only). The secret protection matrix is in `SECRET_PROTECTION_MATRIX.md` (DM-only).

This file is player-safe in structure but should not be read aloud to the player — it is a DM navigation tool.

---

## Revelation Phase Gate Summary

| REV ID | Earliest Phase | Level Band | Prereqs | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| REV_001 | Early | 1–4 | None | Surface revelation; available from session 1 |

## Phase Definitions

| Phase | Level Band | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Early | 1–4 | Local trouble; first mysteries; survival and faction hooks |
| Mid | 5–10 | Regional/factional consequences; historical mysteries unlocking |
| Late | 10–15 | Central conflict visible; major truths within reach |
| Endgame | 16–20 | Final truths; campaign-ending decisions |

## Safe Foreshadowing Before Gate

Revelations may be hinted obliquely before their gate is reached. Oblique hints are:
- Echoes (rite failures, restless dead, old road corruption) with no explanation
- Rumors that create questions without answering them
- Faction behaviour that seems strange but is not yet explained
- NPC partial knowledge (suspicion, not confirmation)
- Physical evidence with no readable interpretation yet

What is NOT safe before the gate:
- Explicit confirmation of any DM-only truth
- NPC dialogue that names the hidden mechanism
- Quest text that explains the central secret
- Player-facing summaries that spoil the apex truth

See `SECRET_PROTECTION_MATRIX.md` for specific unsafe phrasing.
