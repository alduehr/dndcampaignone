## AI Use

**DM-ONLY. Never summarize to the player.** For each load-bearing NPC: what they **know**, **suspect**, **misunderstand**, **hide**; what they reveal **freely / for trust-payment-leverage / only under pressure / never willingly**; the clue/REV they point to; the false leads they may create; and their phase gate. This complements `../08_npcs/NPC_SECRET_LEDGER.md` (the secret-routing table) by adding the *knowledge granularity* the AI DM needs to run a conversation fairly.

**Knowledge-distribution principle:** knowledge is **partial, local, biased, and often mistaken.** Only a handful of NPCs are close to central truths, and those are phase-gated. Most NPCs hold a *piece* and may misread it. Never let one NPC be the only path to any major revelation (the three-clue rule lives in `REVELATION_MAP.md`).

> **Reveal columns:** FREE = volunteers it · TRUST = needs rapport/payment/leverage/a prior clue · PRESSURE = only when cornered/threatened/exposed · NEVER = will not willingly say (must be proven another way).

## Far-Continent NPCs (corroboration only; NONE knows the apex)

Per `../08_npcs/NPC_SECRET_LEDGER.md` (Stage 9.5 section): far majors carry *oblique gated fragments* (the old-songs folk-fragment, the relic trade, the cosmic "thinning" echo, the hopeful grove-contrast) but **none knows the harvest mechanism, the surviving Custodians, the deliberate Quietfall as intent, or the Hollowmere keystone.**
- **Hollin Vane (Heartlands):** apex-*adjacent* — would master a harvest *record*, never the live machine; never confirms REV_007.
- **Mira Cindra (Emberfell), Hadwin Vael (Verdance), Wenna Stone (Karran):** scholar/turnable figures who contribute REV_005-oblique *fragments*; protect their heresy/turn.
- **Wind-Singer Esha, Sun-Singer Doll:** deeply oblique REV_009 cosmic echoes; never name the Quiet Country/harvest.
- **Reveal:** mostly FREE (their region's surface trouble) / TRUST (their gated fragment). **Phase:** Acts 3-4; always oblique.
