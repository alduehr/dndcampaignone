## AI Use

**DM-ONLY TEMPLATE — do not populate here.** This is the blank, instructional template the AI DM copies into runtime (or maintains alongside `../02_runtime_state/KNOWN_CLUES.md` / `HIDDEN_CLUES.md`) to track the mystery state during live play. Keep it current at session end (per `../16_ai_session_packs/SESSION_END_UPDATE_CHECKLIST.md`). It answers, at a glance: *what does the player actually know, what did they miss, what false beliefs are live, which secrets are at risk, and what's the next fair clue door?*

> **At campaign start, every clue is `hidden`, no REV is confirmed, no false lead is active, mystery phase = Early. Do not pre-fill — the live tracker starts empty.**

## Update Cadence

- **Live (in your head):** during play, note discoveries and misreads.
- **Session end:** fill Clues Discovered/Missed, REV status, false-lead status, NPC reveals, faction alerts, secrets-at-risk; sync with `KNOWN_CLUES.md` (player-safe, with the player's *interpretation*) and `HIDDEN_CLUES.md` (DM-only, undiscovered set).
- **Phase change:** when prerequisite REVs are confirmed, advance the phase and re-check `REVELATION_MAP.md` gates and `SECRET_PROTECTION_MATRIX.md`.
