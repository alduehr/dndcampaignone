## Purpose

**DM-ONLY.** The complete network of mysteries in the campaign — how they connect to each other, to factions, to NPCs, and to the central hidden truth. The AI DM uses this to understand which mysteries are interlinked and to avoid accidentally revealing one mystery through another.

**PLACEHOLDER — Not yet populated.** To be established during Stage 1.

## AI Use

Load when beginning a new act, when the player makes a major investigative breakthrough, or when checking whether a clue might inadvertently reveal a connected mystery too early. Do not summarize to player.
