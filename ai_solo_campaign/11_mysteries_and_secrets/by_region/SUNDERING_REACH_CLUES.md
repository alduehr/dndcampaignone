## AI Use

**DM-ONLY. Never summarize to the player.** The runnable clue trail for the starting region — what the player can learn here, by stage, with the per-clue alt-paths and "what if missed." The Reach is the **center of the harvest network**, so it carries the most clue weight and the only paths to the apex. Cross-reference `../CLUE_INDEX.md` (clue IDs) and `../REVELATION_MAP.md` (REV gates). For Act 1 pacing see `../ACT_1_CLUE_TRAILS.md`.

**Phase ceiling:** the Reach delivers REV_001 freely (Act 1), REV_002/REV_003/REV_004 (Act 2), REV_005/REV_006/REV_009 (Act 3, gated), and is the **only** place that can deliver REV_007/REV_008/REV_010 (Act 4-5, under-shrine). Never surface an apex clue out of phase — give fragments and lethal-telegraphing instead.

## Mid-Stage Clues (Act 2, Lvl 5-8) — deliver REV_002, REV_003, REV_004

### REV_002 (the substance)
- **C_SR_011** — a Remembrance relic (Peat Chapel altar; "old glass" from the Deep Adit; Marlow's mislabeled cellar relic). Study it: Arcana/Religion DC 15. It *stores a person.*
- **C_SR_012** — the Gravecallers (Tallow Man / Knell). Forbidden contact: they explain what the dead taught them.
- **C_SR_013** — the Ledger's Hollowmere vault relic. Turn Domic Sael, or run the Ledger Vault heist (`THE_LEDGER_VAULT.md`).
- **Fallback:** if the player avoids relics and the cult, Caradril's commercial M3 paths (C_CAR_005/006) and far "old glass" (C_FC_004) still deliver it.

### REV_003 (Reke steers the basin)
- **C_SR_014** — Reeve Coalmont's suspicions + the council votes (Insight DC 13).
- **C_SR_015** — Pevin Oss's fabricated water-rights filings (Investigation DC 14; protect Pevin Oss — he is scared).
- **C_SR_016** — tail Reke to the basin shore at night (Stealth/Perception).
- **C_SR_017** — Domic/Bly/Marlow funding grumbles ("it's not really about water").
- **C_SR_018** — Magister-Reeve Sela Coalmont: the Compact is "steered against the Reach" (lawful authority path).
- **Ceiling:** banks "Reke is a traitor serving a hidden master." NEVER "his master is the Court" (that is REV_007).

### REV_004 (you were steered)
- **C_SR_019** — press Sefra Quick (she's uneasy about the money; confesses she doesn't know whom she served).
- **C_SR_020** — trace the patron's payments to a cutout (Investigation; the Reach side of the trail).
- **C_SR_021** — the player notices the pattern of being pointed at the shrine (DM confirms what they can prove).
- **Ceiling:** the cutout traces toward Reke-adjacent; the ultimate employer (the Court) stays murky.

## Deep-Stage Clues (Act 3, Lvl 9-12) — deliver REV_005, REV_006, REV_009 (gated)

> **Gate hard.** In Act 1-2 these sites/NPCs give *fragments and atmosphere only.* The full truth is Act 3.

- **C_SR_022** — Warden Othetha breaks her oath (late, trust-gated): the Concord harvested the dead (REV_005). Alt early-but-incomplete: Warden-Confessor Voss is near-M6 and defying the order's silence — a *fault-line* fragment, not the whole.
- **C_SR_023** — the Tallow Man channels a Quietfall-era witness: firsthand harvest + pause (REV_005/REV_006). Sister Knell fears this source; it has its own agenda (handle as a distorted-but-sincere reveal).
- **C_SR_024** — Wend's fully decoded old songs ("the Concord ate the dead") (REV_005). The Reach anchor of the old-songs network.
- **C_SR_025** — Othetha: the Wardens were seeded to keep a *pause* (REV_006).
- **C_SR_026** — Concord Script inscriptions at the shrine/node-sites state the pause (REV_006; Script-gated via Briss/Tamsin Orr).
- **C_SR_034/C_SR_035** — "thin" Harrowgast miners + thin-born; the dead name "the empty country" (REV_009).
- **Convergence note:** the Reach can deliver REV_005/REV_006 without Caradril (Othetha + the Tallow Man + Wend), so a player who never travels to the city is never blocked — Caradril is the *clearest* documentary path, not the only one.

## Late-Stage / Apex Clues (Act 4-5, Lvl 13-20) — REV_007, REV_008, REV_010

> **The Reach is the only place the apex truth lives.** Never deliver before the gate.

- **C_SR_027** — the under-shrine (Drowned Keystone, straight down beneath Hollowmere): meet the Hollow Court (REV_007). Lethal-telegraphed at every prior level (drowning, cold, dread).
- **C_SR_028** — convergence: REV_003 + REV_005 + REV_006 assemble into "surviving Custodians restarting the harvest."
- **C_SR_029** — "the ones who would not pass" (Tallow Man's dead) or Maire reaching out.
- **C_SR_030/031** — Gravecaller and Warden ending-cost warnings (REV_008).
- **C_SR_032/033/036/037/038** — Veyl's case, Maire's heresy, Court internal split (REV_008/REV_010).

## What NOT To Reveal in the Reach (phase-gated)

- Act 1: no REV_002+. Relics are "wrong," not "a substance"; Reke is "hiding something," not "a traitor."
- Act 2: no REV_005+. The conspiracy is contemporary/human, not a century-old machine.
- Act 3: no REV_007. The Concord's sin and the deliberate Quietfall, yes; the *surviving* Custodians, only hinted.
- Always: never name the Hollow Court, the Concord Deep, or the Under-Shrine as confirmed reality before Act 4.
