## AI Use

**DM-ONLY.** The far continent exists to show the player that **the problem is bigger than the northwest** — without ever explaining the keystone mechanism, the Concord Deep, or the Hollow Court. Every far clue is **corroboration only**: it deepens a theme the player has already (or will) confirm in the home regions; it is never the *sole* path to any REV and never delivers the apex. **CRITICAL: no far NPC knows the harvest mechanism, the surviving Custodians, the deliberate Quietfall (as intent), or the Hollowmere keystone.** Far echoes are arc-optional; the endgame stays vertical, under Hollowmere.

## Apex-adjacent far figure (extreme care)

- **Hollin Vane (Concord Heartlands)** is the far figure most likely to stumble on the truth *from the wrong end.* He would try to **master** a harvest-record, not destroy it. The Crown's deepest secret is a **record of what the Concord did** — NEVER the living machine, the Court, or the keystone. Use him as a bad-ending escalation lever and a REV_005 documentary corroborator. He never confirms REV_007. See `../SECRET_PROTECTION_MATRIX.md`.

## Discovery paths far-continent play uses

A player wandering far (Acts 3-4, Lvl 8-17) still progresses the central mystery via:
- **Documentary (Heartlands/Glassmere):** the Ruin'd Crown record + Remnant grand-chapter archives corroborate REV_005 (never REV_007).
- **Mercantile (Emberfell/Karran/Glassmere):** continent-wide relic trade corroborates REV_002.
- **Folk (old-songs network):** the "grey hands" fragment corroborates REV_005 (folk-only).
- **Cosmic (Steppe/Sunmark):** the thinning echoes corroborate REV_009 (deeply oblique).
- **Hopeful (Sunmark):** a working faith corroborates the synthesis (REV_008/REV_010).

**Redundancy guarantee:** none of the above is required. A player who never leaves the NW reaches every REV through the home regions. The far echoes only make the truth feel *continental* and add corroboration paths.

## What NOT to reveal far from home (always)

- The Hollow Court, the Concord Deep, the Under-Shrine / Drowned Keystone, or that the live machine is under Hollowmere.
- That the deliberate Quietfall was the surviving Custodians' act (far sources give the *sin*, never the *survivors*).
- Any "second keystone," rival Hollow Court, or foreign harvest-heart. The conspiracy is **local and singular**.
- The pre-Concord echoes (Saltmere/Marrowdowns/Karran Old Iron forts) as nodes — they are echoes, never the network.
