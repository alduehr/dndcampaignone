## AI Use

**DM-ONLY.** The Tollwood's clue trail. Its identity is **the bargain** — woodfolk customs (offer at the toll-shrines, never cut marked trees) keep a *pre-Concord* deep-wood presence (the Old Mast) asleep. Tollwood clues corroborate REV_001 (the deep wood "going wrong") and carry the oblique **"grey lords" half-memory** of REV_005 — distinct from the Concord proper. **The Old Mast is a pre-Concord landmark-power, NOT a node, NOT the Court.** Never let it become the keystone or name the harvest mechanism.

## Deep clue (Act 3, gated)

- **C_TW_002** — Goodwife Sennet (Coldhearth forest-elder): the oblique **"grey lords took the dead before the roads fell"** half-memory (REV_005). A pre-Concord witness to *something* that took the dead — gated, never the mechanism, never the Court. Corroborated by Warden Brother Ošric (forest dead are *older* than the harvest; resist his rites) and the Old Mast (gated dungeon).

## Local Tollwood mysteries

- **The bargain** (`Q_THE_BARGAIN`) — keeping the Old Mast asleep; a real choice with consequences.
- **The marked oaks / the hanging oaks** — pre-Concord grove + a Gravecaller cell (M3/forbidden door).
- **The toll-war** (`Q_THE_TOLL_WAR`) — Cady Renn (turnable) vs. Skell (murderous); a non-mystery local conflict that also gates safe travel.
- **The drowned vault** (Greenward Toll-Station) — a Concord road-node (M2 fragment).

## Rumors that point toward clues

- "The deep wood's gone wrong out east." (true; -> REV_001)
- "Never cut a marked tree; the wood remembers." (true-flavored; -> the bargain / Old Mast)
- "The old folk say grey lords took the dead before the roads fell." (gated half-truth; -> REV_005 oblique, Act 3 only)

## What NOT to reveal in the Tollwood (phase-gated)

- The Old Mast is **pre-Concord**, NOT a harvest node and NOT the Court; the network bends *around* it.
- The "grey lords" fragment is Act 3, oblique, and never resolves into "the Concord's harvest" here — that resolution belongs to the documentary/Reach paths.
- Never imply the deep-wood presence is the keystone or a second Hollow Court.
