## AI Use

**DM-ONLY.** Caradril is the campaign's **library, bank, and battlefield** — the clearest *documentary* path to the historical truth and the easiest *commercial* path to the substance. It deepens the existing M2/M3/M4/M6/M8/M9 chains without changing any answer. **Act guardrail: Caradril NEVER names the Hollow Court (M7/REV_007).** It delivers REV_005/REV_006 (the Concord's sin, the deliberate Quietfall) at Act 3 / Lvl 9-12, and fragments earlier. Because the same truths are reachable in the Reach, a player who never comes to Caradril is never blocked.
