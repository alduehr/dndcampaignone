# MYSTERY_CHAINS.md

## AI Use

Index/navigation wrapper for the full mystery chain infrastructure. Load this to orient the AI DM before any mystery-sensitive scene. Follow links below to the authoritative files for each chain layer. This file does not contain secrets — all DM-only material lives in the linked files.

---

## Overview

The campaign's mystery chains operate on multiple levels simultaneously. A player may advance any chain in any order; no chain is a strict prerequisite for another except where the REVELATION_MAP defines a prereq (see REV_ entries).

| Chain Layer | What it covers | Primary file |
|---|---|---|
| Mystery web (all chains, DM-only) | Full mystery hierarchy: surface → endgame | `MYSTERY_WEB.md` (dm-only) |
| Revelation map (DM-only) | All 10 major revelations, prereqs, clue sources, phase gates | `REVELATION_MAP.md` (dm-only) |
| Clue index (dm-only) | ~100 clues, sources, retrieval paths, redundancy | `CLUE_INDEX.md` (dm-only) |
| Act 1 clue trails (dm-only) | Level 1–4 clue access overlay, R1 cap | `ACT_1_CLUE_TRAILS.md` (dm-only) |
| Regional clue trails (mixed) | Per-region clue lists for AI DM retrieval | `by_region/` (6 files below) |
| Discovery paths (mixed) | 10 playstyle-specific investigation paths | `DISCOVERY_PATHS.md` |
| False leads (mixed) | 9 recoverable misdirections | `FALSE_LEADS_AND_MISDIRECTIONS.md` |
| Faction knowledge (mixed/dm-only) | What each faction knows, hides, or misunderstands | `by_faction/FACTION_KNOWLEDGE_MAP.md` |
| NPC knowledge (mixed) | Per-NPC knowledge, suspicion, hiding, phase gates | `NPC_KNOWLEDGE_MAP.md` |

| Reveal timing (mixed) | Phase gates and earliest-reveal chart | `REVEAL_TIMING.md` |

## Mystery Chain Summary (player-safe framing)

The world is full of death-rite failures, restless dead, and ruins that hum with old purpose. What happened to the Custodian Concord? Why do the Ashen Wardens seal the shrines they claim to protect? What is in the relics the Cinder Ledger harvests so carefully? These questions connect. The answers are earned through play.

*(Full chain structure and hidden answers are in `MYSTERY_WEB.md` and `REVELATION_MAP.md` — DM-only.)*
