## Summary

Nine recoverable false leads are documented in `FALSE_LEADS_AND_MISDIRECTIONS.md` (FL_001 through FL_009). Each:

- Has a plausible surface explanation
- Is supported by real in-world evidence
- Benefits an identifiable faction or NPC
- Points toward a useful partial truth even while misdirecting
- Can be disproven through investigation, social interaction, or exploration
- Must not be a pure dead end — every false lead teaches something

False leads are fair. The player should be able to recognize, recover from, and learn from every one.
