# _PLACEHOLDER.md — /07_factions/minor_factions/

## Purpose

This folder will contain files for minor factions — local guilds, small cults, town councils, minor noble houses, local gangs, small religious orders, and other organizations too small for major faction treatment.

## Current Status

**Empty — Stage 0 scaffold.** Minor factions will be added during Stage 3 and later passes.

## Expected Contents

Minor faction entries can be brief compared to major factions, but should still include:

- Public face
- True agenda (if hidden)
- Leader or key member
- Methods and resources
- How they connect to larger factions
- One or two hooks
- What they do if ignored

Minor factions may also appear as sections within settlement files rather than standalone files.
