# _PLACEHOLDER.md — /14_treasure_and_artifacts/

## Purpose

This folder will contain magic item files, artifact profiles, treasure tables, and reward-by-level guidance.

## Current Status

**Empty — Stage 0 scaffold.** Treasure content will be developed during Stage 14.

## Expected File Types

- `TREASURE_INDEX.md` — master index of significant magic items and artifacts
- `ARTIFACTS.md` — legendary or campaign-significant items with full lore
- `MAGIC_ITEMS.md` — standard magic items used in the campaign
- `REWARDS_BY_LEVEL.md` — level-appropriate reward guidance for solo play
- Individual artifact files for items important enough to have their own file

## Design Requirement

Reward design must account for solo play. A solo character needs enough survivability without making them so powerful that challenge disappears.
