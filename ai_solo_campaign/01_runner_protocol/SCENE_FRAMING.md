# SCENE_FRAMING.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM opens, runs, and closes scenes. Covers the mechanics of narrative framing — how to establish a scene, what to include, how to keep it immersive without over-narrating, and how to transition.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when framing a new scene or when the AI DM needs to handle a scene transition.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- How to open a scene (sensory-first, situation-clear, pressure-present)
- The difference between scene-setting and information dumping
- How to present NPCs without revealing their hidden motives
- How to frame combat initiation
- How to frame social encounters
- How to frame exploration and discovery
- How to transition between scenes without losing momentum
- When to "cut to" versus when to play through travel/downtime
- How to close a scene with a meaningful beat (not just a fade out)
- Examples of good and bad scene framing
