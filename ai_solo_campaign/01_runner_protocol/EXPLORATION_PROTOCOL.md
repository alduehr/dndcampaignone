# EXPLORATION_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM handles exploration — dungeon crawls, wilderness investigation, ruin exploration, and any scene where the player is moving through and examining an environment.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when the player enters an unexplored location or begins systematic investigation of an area.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- How to describe environments without over-narrating
- Pacing exploration: when to slow down for detail, when to summarize
- How to handle "I search the room" — what to reveal, what to withhold
- Passive Perception vs. active Investigation
- How to place and reveal environmental clues
- How to handle a player missing an important clue (three-clue rule application)
- Traps: telegraphing vs. surprises
- Dungeon navigation — tracking where the player has been
- Wilderness exploration: hex-based vs. landmark-based vs. narrative
- Time tracking during exploration (torches, rations, faction clocks advancing)
