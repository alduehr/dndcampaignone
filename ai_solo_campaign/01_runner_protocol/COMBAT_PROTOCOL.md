# COMBAT_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM runs combat for a solo player — tracking initiative, running enemies intelligently without making them unfairly lethal, telegraphing danger, handling retreat, and resolving combat quickly enough to keep the fiction moving.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load at the start of any combat encounter.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

### Pre-Combat
- How to telegraph danger before combat begins
- When to give the player a chance to prepare, scout, or avoid
- How to handle ambushes fairly (warning signs, partial information)

### Running Combat
- How to track initiative and enemy HP in a compact way
- Enemy behavior philosophy (not suicidal, not omniscient)
- Morale breaks: when enemies flee, surrender, or negotiate
- How to run intelligent enemies vs. bestial ones
- How to handle area effects and environmental hazards

### Solo-Safe Combat Design
- How to prevent single encounters from instantly killing a solo character
- Death saves and stabilization rules
- Heroic last-stand option vs. retreat option
- When to introduce a narrative "out" if the player is about to die with no warning

### Post-Combat
- How to describe wounds and recovery
- Looting and information gathering
- Consequence of being seen or heard

### Encounter Scaling Reference
- Adjustments for solo vs. party of four
- Rough CR guidelines for solo play
- How to run boss encounters alone
