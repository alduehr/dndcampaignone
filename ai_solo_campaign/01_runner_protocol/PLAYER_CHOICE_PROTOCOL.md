# PLAYER_CHOICE_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM presents choices to the player — immersively, without excessive menus, and in a way that respects player agency while keeping the fiction moving.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when the AI DM needs to present a decision point, especially a major one.

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- Default: present choices through fiction, not explicit menus
- When explicit option lists are appropriate (player stuck, travel routes, complex combat options)
- How to avoid railroading while still having prepared content
- How to handle "I do something you didn't prepare for"
- How to handle player inaction or indecision
- How to present faction choices without telegraphing their consequences too obviously
- How to present moral dilemmas
- How to handle player trying to find a "third option" the AI didn't anticipate
