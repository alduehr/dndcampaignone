# TRAVEL_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM handles overland and regional travel — journey pacing, random events, faction activity en route, terrain and weather, travel encounters, and arriving at a new location.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when the player begins a significant journey between locations.

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- Travel time calculation (reference MAP_DESCRIPTION.md)
- When to play travel in detail vs. summarize
- Travel encounter tables: how to use them without being random noise
- Purposeful travel events: encounters that advance quests, deliver rumors, or develop NPCs
- Weather and terrain as interesting complications, not just flavor
- Faction patrols, checkpoints, and road dangers by region
- What the player can do during travel (foraging, scouting, long rests)
- How time passing during travel advances world clocks
- Arriving at a new location: first impression scene framing
- "You get there without incident" — when to use it and when not to
