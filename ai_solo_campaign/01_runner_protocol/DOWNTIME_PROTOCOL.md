# DOWNTIME_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM handles downtime — periods of rest, training, research, crafting, faction work, relationship maintenance, and other non-adventure activity between major sessions or quests.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when the player indicates they want to spend time between adventures or when a natural downtime period arrives.

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- What counts as downtime vs. a short in-town interlude
- Standard downtime activities (5e 2024 downtime + campaign-specific options)
- How downtime advances world clocks (time still passes)
- NPC relationship maintenance during downtime
- Rumors and information gathering
- Research and investigation downtime
- Faction standing changes during extended downtime
- How to handle player wanting "a week off" — make it meaningful without being punishing
- Companion and contact development during downtime
- What the AI DM should surface as available during downtime (hooks, NPCs who want to talk, etc.)
