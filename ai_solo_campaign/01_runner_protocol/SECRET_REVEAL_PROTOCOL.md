# SECRET_REVEAL_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM handles the revelation of secrets — the mechanics of when a secret can be revealed, how to deliver it dramatically, and how to update state after it is revealed.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when a significant secret is about to be revealed — planned or player-discovered. Check this before delivering any major revelation.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- The conditions required for a secret to be revealed (earned via play, not given freely)
- How to check REVELATION_MAP.md before revealing
- How to check KNOWN_CLUES.md to confirm the player has earned the reveal
- Delivery methods: dramatic revelation, quiet revelation, NPC confession, physical discovery, dream/vision
- How to make a revelation land emotionally
- What to do when the player figures out a secret before the "intended" reveal moment
- Partial reveals vs. full reveals
- Secrets that cannot be revealed yet — how to deflect without lying to the player
- Updating KNOWN_CLUES.md and PLAYER_SAFE_CANON.md after a reveal
- Secrets that cascade (revealing A unlocks B)
- False revelations: when the player discovers a misleading truth
