# SOCIAL_SCENE_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM runs social encounters — NPC conversations, negotiations, persuasion attempts, deception, interrogation, and social politics. Covers how to roleplay NPCs consistently with their established personality and secrets.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when running a meaningful social scene, especially with a major or secondary NPC.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- How to voice NPCs consistently with established voice notes
- How to roleplay NPC attitudes (hostile, wary, friendly, etc.) through dialogue
- When to call for Persuasion / Deception / Insight rolls vs. playing it out
- How to handle NPC lies — keep them consistent, track what was said
- What NPCs will and won't reveal at each attitude level
- Social failure handling (NPC becomes suspicious, ends conversation, alerts others)
- NPC memory: how to use `NPC_MEMORY.md` during a social scene
- Faction-colored NPC behavior (how faction membership affects NPC goals)
- When an NPC should be movable and when they should be immovable
- Reading social scenes for information delivery vs. relationship building vs. quest advancement
