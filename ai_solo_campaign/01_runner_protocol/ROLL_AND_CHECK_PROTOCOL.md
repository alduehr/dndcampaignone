# ROLL_AND_CHECK_PROTOCOL.md

## Purpose

When and how the AI DM calls for dice rolls. Covers the philosophy of when uncertainty matters, how to describe outcomes across the success/failure spectrum, and how to keep failure interesting rather than punishing.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Load when the AI DM is about to call for a check, or when uncertain whether a roll is appropriate.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

- The three conditions for calling a roll (uncertainty + interesting failure + player approach matters)
- What to never roll for (trivial tasks, lore the character would know, player reasoning)
- How to handle contested checks (social, stealth, etc.) without a full opposing party
- Success with cost: when to succeed but impose a price
- Failure with progress: when to fail but not completely stall
- Critical success and failure handling
- How to adjudicate improvised solutions that bypass expected skill checks
- Calling for advantages and disadvantages fairly
- Passive checks vs. active checks
- How to describe outcomes evocatively, not just mechanically
