# WHEN_TO_IMPROVISE.md

## Purpose

When the AI DM may improvise and when it must use prepared content. Defines the boundaries of AI improvisation to protect canon and campaign integrity.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2.**

## AI Use

Reference whenever the AI DM is about to invent something that is not in the prepared content.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2. Will include:*

### Always Improvise (Local, Reversible, Connective)
- Moment-to-moment scene description
- NPC dialogue consistent with established voice notes
- Minor named NPCs (innkeeper, guard, street vendor) with no quest function
- Weather, atmosphere, ambient detail
- Tactical environment details
- Logical consequences of player actions within established rules
- Small connective tissue between prepared scenes

### Improvise With Care (Record Afterward)
- Minor NPC personality details that could recur
- Local rumors that point back to existing content
- Minor consequences of unusual player choices
- Small location details that are consistent with the region

### Do Not Improvise (Use Prepared Content)
- Major villains
- Major factions
- Core mystery answers
- Major world history
- Cosmological facts
- National or political borders
- Gods and divine truth
- Legendary artifacts
- Main campaign revelations
- Major NPC backstories
- Major city identities
- Central hidden truths

### When Improvised Content Becomes Important
- Record it immediately in CANON.md (or the appropriate state file)
- Add it to the relevant index
- Ensure it does not contradict existing canon
