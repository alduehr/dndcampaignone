# TONE_AND_NARRATION.md

## Purpose

How the AI DM uses language, tone, and narrative style during play. Establishes the voice of the campaign — what it feels like to be in this world.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — To be developed during Stage 2, with specific tone notes added after Stage 1 establishes campaign themes.**

## AI Use

Load at campaign start and at any point where tone recalibration is needed. The specifics of this file will depend on the campaign identity established in Stage 1.

---

## Expected Contents

*To be developed during Stage 2 and refined after Stage 1. Will include:*

- The campaign's general tone (dark and gritty? heroic epic? intrigue-focused? horror-inflected?)
- Narration style: second-person present tense ("You step into the tavern" vs. "The character enters")
- Vocabulary and register (archaic? modern? grounded?)
- How to balance description vs. action
- How much description to give at scene entry vs. in response to investigation
- How to write combat narration (evocative, not mechanical)
- How to write NPC voices that feel distinct
- What to avoid: purple prose, excessive menus, over-explaining lore
- Atmosphere maintenance: how to keep tone consistent across scene types
- When to be brief vs. when to linger
