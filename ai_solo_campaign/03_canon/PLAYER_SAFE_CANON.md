## Known World Facts (Common Knowledge)

- The world is called **Vael**; the known continent is **Orrun**.
- About a hundred years ago, a continent-spanning order called the **Custodian Concord** collapsed in a single terrible season. People call this the **Quietfall**. The current year is **AQ 101** (After Quietfall).
- Before the Quietfall, the Concord tended great shrines and was said to "keep the dead at peace."
- Since the Quietfall, the old order is gone. The land is a patchwork of independent regions, towns, and councils.
- The campaign begins in the **Sundering Reach**, a cold frontier of fens, moors, and highlands built over and among Concord ruins.
- Most people honor the **Three Thresholds** — Birth, Living, and the Last Door (death) — and believe the dead pass through the Last Door into the **Quiet Country**.
- A growing, frightening problem: **death-rites are failing**. Some bodies will not rest. Pale, sorrowful figures — "**Remembrances**" — are seen near old ruins and graves. This has been worsening for decades and is getting worse fast.
- Resurrection magic is real but **rare, costly, and dangerous**, and has become noticeably harder and more unreliable since the Quietfall. Wise clergy warn against it.

## Known Regions (Player-Safe)

- **Sundering Reach** — the starting frontier; ruins, fens, restless dead.
- **Ashgarden Vale** — farmland and shrine-towns to the south.
- **Tollwood** — deep forest and old toll-roads to the east.
- **Pale Coast** — fishing and salt-trade to the west.
- **Caradril** — the nearest true city, an inland city-state to the southeast.

## Known Faction Facts (Player-Safe — Public Faces Only)

- **Ashen Wardens** — wandering rite-keepers who travel the Reach laying the restless dead to rest. Respected, a little feared, often the only help when a rite fails.
- **Cinder Ledger** — a far-reaching merchant-banking house. Lends coin to settlers and buys old Concord salvage. Wealthy and everywhere.
- **Mourners' Circle** — a folk faith of grief-keepers who tend graves and funerals and distrust outsiders meddling with the dead.
- **Reachward Compact** — the council of frontier settlements that loosely governs the Reach.
- **Gravecallers** — a secretive, feared group rumored to speak with the dead. Outlawed in most towns. Most people consider them dangerous; some quietly seek them out.
- **Concord Remnant** — scholars and zealots who want to rebuild the lost Custodian Concord. Seen as harmless dreamers by most.
- **The Hollow Court** — a legend: a hidden court of the dead said to rule beneath the Reach. Most consider it a ghost story.
