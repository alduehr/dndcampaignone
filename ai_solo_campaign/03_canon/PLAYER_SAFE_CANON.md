## Purpose

All world facts that are safe to share with or in the presence of the player. This includes public knowledge, common beliefs, in-world rumors that are true, and facts the player character would reasonably know. Updated as discoveries are made and as player-safe facts are established.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — Not yet populated.**

---

## What Belongs Here

- Public facts about the world (geography, history, common knowledge)
- Faction public faces
- NPC public identities and reputations
- Laws, customs, religious beliefs as known to the public
- Facts the player character has confirmed through play
- Information the player has discovered and earned

## What Does NOT Belong Here

- Hidden faction agendas
- True causes of events
- Villain identities
- Secret NPC motives
- Mystery answers
- DM-only cosmological truths
- Future events
