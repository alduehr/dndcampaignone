# START_NEW_CAMPAIGN_PROMPT.md

## Purpose

The prompt the AI DM uses to begin the very first session of the campaign. Loads all necessary context, establishes the opening scene, and introduces the player to the world without spoiling hidden truths.

## Current Status

**PLACEHOLDER — Not yet populated.** To be created during Stage 2 once Stage 1 has established the campaign world and starting situation.

---

## When To Use

Use this file exactly once — at the start of the first session, before any play has occurred. After session 1, use `RESUME_CAMPAIGN_PROMPT.md` instead.

---

## Expected Contents

When created, this file should contain:

### Files To Load Before Session
List of files the AI DM must load before delivering the opening.

### Pre-Session Checklist
- Confirm player character is set up in `PLAYER_CHARACTER.md`
- Confirm starting location is set in `CURRENT_LOCATION.md`
- Confirm opening scene is in `CURRENT_SCENE.md`
- Confirm active quests / opening hooks are in `ACTIVE_QUESTS.md`
- Confirm world clocks are initialized in `WORLD_CLOCKS.md`

### Opening Scene
- Immersive second-person opening narration
- What the player sees, hears, smells
- Who is present
- Immediate situation and pressure
- Choices visible through the fiction

### Opening Moves
- 2–3 natural openings the AI DM can lead with based on player response
- What to do if the player asks about character background
- What to do if the player goes somewhere unexpected immediately

### What Not To Reveal Yet
- Hidden truths to protect at session start
- Which NPCs' secrets must stay hidden
- What the AI DM must not hint at yet

### DM-Only Opening Notes
- Hidden forces at work during the opening scene
- What each present faction/NPC actually wants
- The first foreshadowing opportunity
