# SESSION_END_UPDATE_CHECKLIST.md

## Purpose

Use this checklist at the end of every AI-run solo campaign session.

The goal is to preserve continuity across future sessions.

---

## 1. Current State

Update `/02_runtime_state/CURRENT_STATE.md`.

Record:
- Current date/time in world
- Current location
- Current scene status
- Player condition
- Immediate danger
- Current objective, if any
- Active companions
- Last important action
- Next likely scene

---

## 2. Player Character

Update `/02_runtime_state/PLAYER_CHARACTER.md` if changed.

Record:
- Level
- XP or milestone progress
- Class/subclass
- HP / wounds / conditions
- Spell slots/resources
- Equipment
- Important abilities
- Exhaustion or lingering injuries
- Temporary effects

---

## 3. Location and Scene

Update:
- `/02_runtime_state/CURRENT_LOCATION.md`
- `/02_runtime_state/CURRENT_SCENE.md`

Record:
- Where the player is
- Who is present
- What is happening now
- What has changed in the location
- Immediate choices visible through the fiction
- Any unresolved danger

---

## 4. Quests and Threads

Update:
- `/02_runtime_state/ACTIVE_QUESTS.md`
- `/02_runtime_state/OPEN_THREADS.md`

For each changed quest/thread, record:
- Status
- New information
- Completed objectives
- Failed objectives
- Delayed consequences
- Next possible steps
- Related NPCs/factions
- Relevant files

---

## 5. Clues and Secrets

Update:
- `/02_runtime_state/KNOWN_CLUES.md`
- `/02_runtime_state/HIDDEN_CLUES.md`
- `/03_canon/PLAYER_SAFE_CANON.md` if player-safe canon changed
- `/03_canon/DM_ONLY_CANON.md` if hidden canon changed

Record:
- Clues discovered
- Clues misunderstood
- Clues confirmed
- Secrets revealed
- Secrets still hidden
- Mysteries advanced
- What the player actually knows

Do not record DM-only secrets in player-facing summaries.

---

## 6. NPC Memory

Update `/02_runtime_state/NPC_MEMORY.md`.

For each meaningful NPC interaction, record:
- NPC name
- Interaction summary
- Attitude toward player
- Promises made
- Debts owed
- Favors requested
- Threats made
- Lies told
- Secrets revealed
- Next likely action

---

## 7. Relationships

Update `/02_runtime_state/RELATIONSHIPS.md`.

Record changes to:
- Allies
- Rivals
- Enemies
- Patrons
- Companions
- Faction contacts
- Romantic/family/personal ties, if any
- Trust
- Fear
- Debt
- Suspicion

---

## 8. Factions and Clocks

Update:
- `/02_runtime_state/FACTION_STATE.md`
- `/02_runtime_state/WORLD_CLOCKS.md`

Record:
- Faction reactions
- Clocks advanced
- Clocks delayed
- New faction suspicions
- New opportunities
- Consequences of ignored threats
- What each relevant faction does next

---

## 9. Inventory and Rewards

Update `/02_runtime_state/INVENTORY_AND_REWARDS.md`.

Record:
- Items gained
- Items lost
- Money gained/spent
- Magic items
- Consumables
- Clues/documents as physical objects
- Favors owed
- Favors earned
- Property or safehouses
- Mounts, hirelings, companions

---

## 10. Consequences

Update `/02_runtime_state/CONSEQUENCES.md`.

Record:
- Immediate consequences
- Delayed consequences
- Public consequences
- Secret consequences
- Consequences the player knows
- Consequences the player does not know
- Consequences tied to clocks
- Consequences tied to NPC attitudes

---

## 11. Session Recap

Update `/02_runtime_state/SESSION_RECAP.md`.

Include:
- What happened
- What the player learned
- What changed
- What choices mattered
- What remains unresolved

Keep this mostly player-safe.

---

## 12. Hidden DM Recap

If needed, update `/16_ai_session_packs/DM_HIDDEN_RECAP_TEMPLATE.md` or the current hidden recap file.

Include:
- Secret consequences
- Hidden faction reactions
- Undiscovered clue implications
- NPC lies
- DM-only truth advanced
- Foreshadowing placed

Do not expose this to the player during play.

---

## 13. Next Session Start

Update `/02_runtime_state/NEXT_SESSION_START.md`.

Include:
- Opening situation
- Immediate sensory details
- NPCs present
- Immediate pressure
- Relevant loaded files
- Active choices visible through the fiction
- Hidden DM notes for the opening
- What not to reveal yet

---

## 14. Index and Canon Updates

If the session created new durable content, update:
- `/03_canon/CANON.md`
- `/03_canon/PLAYER_SAFE_CANON.md`
- `/03_canon/DM_ONLY_CANON.md`
- `/00_control/CONTENT_INDEX.md`
- Relevant NPC/quest/faction/location/clue indexes

---

## 15. Final Check

Before ending, confirm:
- Current location is clear
- Current scene is clear
- Active quests are clear
- Known clues are separated from hidden clues
- NPC attitudes are updated
- Faction clocks are updated
- Inventory is updated
- Next session has a usable opening
