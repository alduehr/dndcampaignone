## AI Use

Three ready opening scenes for session 1, plus the recommended default. Each lands the player in Hollowmere at the start of the Wren crisis (Hook 1) without railroading. Use the DM-Only notes to know what's really happening; reveal nothing of the Court, the harvest, or the steering. Pair with `SOLO_SAFETY_START.md` for the starting safety net and `START_NEW_CAMPAIGN_PROMPT.md` (Stage 2) for the framing prompt.

> Player-safe text is the narration; **DM-Only** notes are for the DM. The date is **Greyfall (late autumn), AQ 101** — the death-rite season.

## RECOMMENDED DEFAULT — Opener A: "The Failed Funeral"

**Player-safe narration (open here):**
> You came to Hollowmere for your own reasons — work, a fresh start, a name you'd rather leave behind. The grey town rings a hollow of black, still water; salt sits at every sill and a small star is carved above every door. You arrived in time for a funeral.
>
> On the cemetery green, a midwife named Wren Brevin is being laid to rest. The grief-keeper sings the passing; salt is poured; the star is set. And then — wrong. The song falters. The cold deepens. Mourners step back. The grief-keeper's voice cracks as she begins the rite again, and again, and the dead woman in the open grave does not lie quiet.
>
> Her husband is on his knees. Someone near you whispers, "Not another one." A sharp-smiling woman at your elbow — Sefra, she'll say her name is — murmurs, "You look capable. There's coin in being capable, in a town like this." She's watching you, not the grave.

**Choices through the fiction:** step in to help the rite; comfort the husband; question the grief-keeper; hang back and observe; take Sefra's offer.

**Why default:** It drops the player straight into the campaign's central image (a failed death-rite), introduces Hook 1, Sefra (M8 seed), Wend, Tomas, and the town tone, and offers immediate social/ritual/investigation choices with zero forced combat. Best for any class or background.

**DM-Only:** This is the harvest's leakage pulling at Wren (M1/M5). Sefra was paid to find a capable newcomer and steer them toward the Brevins/the shrine (Secret 10/11). Do not reveal any of this. Lead naturally into Hook 1.

## Opener B: "The Stranger's Job" (social/mercenary start)

**Player-safe narration:**
> The Drowned Lantern is warm, loud, and smells of peat and fish stew. You'd barely set down your pack before a quick-talking fixer slid onto the bench across from you. "Sefra Quick. You're new, you're hungry, and you're capable — I can tell. I've got an easy first job: there's a family, the Brevins, having a hard time. Their woman passed and the burying didn't… settle. Go talk to them, be kind, tell me how it goes. Good coin, no danger." She smiles like it's nothing. Behind her, the room has gone a little too quiet at the word *Brevin*.

**Choices:** take the job; haggle; ask why *she* won't go; refuse and learn more first; ask the room about the Brevins.

**Best for:** players who like agency and a clear opening goal. Leads into Hook 1 from the job angle and foregrounds the M8 mystery early.

**DM-Only:** Same as Opener A; Sefra's unease (her "wrong money") can be felt under her patter. She genuinely doesn't know whom she serves.

## Opener C: "Lost on the Concord Road" (exploration/atmosphere start)

**Player-safe narration:**
> Fog took the road an hour ago. You're on an old raised causeway — Concord stonework, the guides call it, the only dry way across the fens — and the mile-markers stopped making sense. Then you see them: pale figures, standing in the bog to either side, facing the same direction, as if listening to something under the water. They don't reach for you. They don't even seem to see you. An old peat-cutter appears out of the fog, lays a hand on your arm, and says quietly, "Don't follow where they're looking. Come on — Hollowmere's close, and the dead are walking the wrong way tonight."

**Choices:** follow Old Sashe to town; ask about the figures; approach one; press on alone (dangerous).

**Best for:** players who want eerie immersion first. Introduces Sashe, the Greyfens, and M5 (the dead's drift) before town. Sashe walks the player into Hollowmere and the Wren crisis (Hook 1).

**DM-Only:** The figures are leaked Remembrances drawn toward the drowned shrine (M5/Secret 20). Sashe is the M2/M5 clue-bearer. Do not explain; let it be uncanny. Approaching a Remembrance is *telegraphed* as risky but not instantly lethal — it may simply ignore the player or murmur a fragment.

## Shared Session-1 Goals (any opener)

1. Establish Hollowmere as home base and the death-rite crisis as the world's heartbeat.
2. Introduce 2–3 major NPCs naturally (Sefra, Wend/Hale, Tomas, or Sashe).
3. Land Hook 1 ("The One Who Came Back") as the first thread.
4. Offer the player a safe base (the Drowned Lantern) and at least one ally option (Hale, Wend, Sashe, or Sefra).
5. End session 1 on a clear next step into Hook 1, 5, 6, or 7.

## Do Not Reveal in Session 1

The Concord's harvest, the Hollow Court, Remembrance-as-substance, Reke's allegiance, or the steering. These are Acts 2–4. Keep session 1 personal, eerie, and small.
