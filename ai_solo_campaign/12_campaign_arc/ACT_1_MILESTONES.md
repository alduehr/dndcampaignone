## AI Use

**Mixed secrecy — DM-facing.** Explicit, earnable-multiple-ways level-up triggers for Act 1. The campaign uses **milestone leveling** (`../03_canon/LEVELING_ASSUMPTIONS.md`); this file makes the milestones concrete and adds **optional XP-style guidance** for DMs who prefer a points feel. **Combat is never required to level** — each milestone lists qualifying paths across combat, social, exploration, investigation, and faction work. Communicate level-ups at a natural rest/reflection beat (after a hard scene, an inn night, a quest's close). Hold Act 1's revelation cap at R1 even at the level 4→5 transition; the *threshold* of the conspiracy is delivered, not the conspiracy's truth.

> **Anti-railroad rule:** a milestone fires when the player achieves *any one* of its qualifying paths — not a specific quest. If the player took an unexpected route, match it to the nearest milestone's *intent* (a closed problem, a chosen direction, a Concord site survived, a basin-stance taken) and level them.

## Optional XP-Style Guidance (for DMs who want a points feel)

Milestone is the default. If using XP, treat Act 1 as ~**0 → ~2,700 XP** (D&D 5e thresholds: L2 ≈ 300, L3 ≈ 900, L4 ≈ 2,700), earned from *any* mix of:

| Activity | Approx. value (L1–4 scale) |
|---|---|
| Resolving a Restless Remembrance (rite or parley) | "encounter" credit ≈ a CR 1/2 win (~100 XP), whether or not a blow is struck |
| A telegraphed combat won/avoided (Threats 1–5) | by CR (1/8 ≈ 25 · 1/4 ≈ 50 · 1/2 ≈ 100 · 1 ≈ 200 · 2 ≈ 450) |
| Completing a developed Act 1 quest (any of the 6 faction quests or a regional quest) | "minor milestone" ≈ 200–400 |
| Cracking a clue / reaching R1 on a mystery route | "discovery" ≈ 100–200 |
| A social victory (turning, allying, de-escalating, winning a vote/scene) | "social encounter" ≈ 100–300 |
| Reaching/surviving a Concord site | "exploration milestone" ≈ 300–450 |

**The point of the table is parity:** a stealth/social/exploration session earns as much as a combat session. Do not require kills. When the running total crosses a threshold (or the milestone trigger below fires), level up — whichever comes first.

## Milestone 1 → 2 (close a first problem)

**Intent:** the player has closed *one* first personal or community problem and is no longer a stranger passing through.

**Earn by ANY of:**
- **Personal/social:** resolve Wren's situation one way or another (released by true rite, sheltered, hidden, fought, or the crisis otherwise concludes) — `Q_ACT1_WARDEN_THE_TRUE_RITE`, Hook 1.
- **Faction/community:** complete a first faction door's opening beat (a Warden rite, a Ledger fetch, a Mourner errand, a Compact look-in, a fen recovery) — any Act 1 quest's first objective.
- **Exploration/investigation:** close an equivalent first community problem elsewhere if the player fled Hollowmere (a Kettle Bridge burial, a Saltmargin salt run, a Greywater rite) — regional quests.

**Unlocks narratively:** the town (or the next town) starts to *know* the player; an ally warms (Hale, Sashe, Halla, Coalmont, Wend); the first faction door is genuinely open.
**New threats relevant:** Restless Remembrances become routine (Threat 1); first frontier toughs (Threat 3).
**Who notices:** Sefra (the patron is "working"); Halla and the town gossip; whichever faction the player helped.

## Milestone 2 → 3 (commit to a direction)

**Intent:** the player has completed a first *real* quest **and** chosen a thread to pull — they have a direction, not just a job.

**Earn by ANY of:**
- **Faction:** finish a developed Act 1 quest and commit to that faction's path (Wardens, Compact, Ledger, Mourners, Gravecallers, or Remnant) — any of the 6 `act_1_quests/`.
- **Political:** take up Coalmont's basin investigation or Reke's errand — picking a *side* on the basin question (Hooks 4/5).
- **Mystery:** lock onto a mystery route in earnest (the drift with Sashe, the relics with the Ledger, the cult's truth) — `ACT_1_CLUE_TRAILS.md` routes A–D.
- **Exploration:** range beyond Hollowmere to a second settlement and take its central quest (the Heights race, the salt crisis, the Holm's failures).

**Unlocks narratively:** the player is now a *player* in the Reach's politics; a faction begins to court or test them; a rival may appear (Tallytooth, Veska Dunn, Cole Ashby).
**New threats relevant:** Wrathful Remembrances begin to appear basin-adjacent (Threat 2); rival salvage crews (Threat 5) if the player ranges north.
**Who notices:** the chosen faction warms; its rivals take note; clocks the player is acting against slow, the ones they ignore tick.

## Milestone 3 → 4 (reach the source's edge)

**Intent:** the player has reached and survived a **first Concord site** and grasped that the failures have a *source* (R1 hardens from "a pattern" to "a place").

**Earn by ANY of:**
- **Exploration/dungeon:** reach and survive a first Concord site — the Peat Chapel, the basin-shore Surfacing Ruin, the Whispering Cairn, the Sunken Tollhouse, or (bolder) the Barrow/Deep Adit — and come away with the *shape* of M2.
- **Investigation:** assemble the R1 picture without a delve — combine 2+ clue routes (Wren + drift, or drift + surveys, or political + shore) so "the basin is the source" is undeniable (`ACT_1_CLUE_TRAILS.md`).
- **Social/forbidden:** sit the Tallow Man's audience and carry away the "door under the water" fragment, *or* earn enough Warden/Mourner trust that an elder confirms "the basin is the heart of it" (R1-capped).

**Unlocks narratively:** the basin is now, unmistakably, the center; the player feels the weight of *something built and drowned*; foreshadowing of Act 2 (relics that "remember," Reke's too-smooth manner, the Ledger's quiet buying) starts to itch.
**New threats relevant:** basin-adjacent sites grow dangerous (telegraphed); the deep basin/shrine is flagged as *not yet survivable* (gated).
**Who notices:** the factions racing for the source (Remnant, Ledger, Gravecaller radicals) treat the player as a competitor; Reke quietly tracks them (DM-Only — do not expose).

## Milestone 4 → 5 (the threshold of the conspiracy — unlocks Act 2)

**Intent:** the player faces a **first faction-level decision about the basin scheme** and the harvest's *reality* becomes undeniable — they stand at the threshold of the conspiracy. (Act 2 content lives in Stage 15 `ACT_2_LEVELS_5_8.md`; Act 1 only delivers the player *here*.)

**Earn by ANY of (multiple triggers — the DM uses whichever the player's path produces):**
1. **Take a side on the basin scheme** — help Reke, oppose him with Coalmont, or move to sway/sabotage the vote (`Q_ACT1_COMPACT_THE_REEVES_DOUBT`, Hooks 4/5). The political door to Act 2's R3.
2. **Commit to learning what the relics/dead truly are** — earnestly study a relic or throw in with the Gravecallers, ready (in Act 2) to learn what Remembrance *is* (R2/M3 — held until Act 2).
3. **Reach the basin shore / a surfacing ruin and confront its scale** — the M2 shape hardens into "this is enormous, intact, and built," and the player resolves to find out what it is.
4. **Clocks force it** — if the player stays passive, #2 Basin Drains and #3 Wardens Break advance until the surfacing shrine and active factions make the conspiracy undeniable on their own (a slower, world-driven trigger; never blocked).

**Unlocks narratively:** Act 2 ("Conspiracy," levels 5–8) — a *human* conspiracy around the dead becomes the frame; R2/R3/R4 come into reach.
**Reveal cap:** still R1 at the *moment* of transition. Deliver the *threshold* (a basin-stance chosen or forced; the source undeniable) — **not** Remembrance-as-substance, Reke's master, the steering employer, the Concord's sin, or the Court.

## Faction Notice By Level (who reacts as the player grows)

| At level | Factions that notice the player's growing influence (player-safe surface) |
|---|---|
| 2 | The faction the player first helped warms; Sefra reports the player is capable; Halla/town gossip spreads their name. |
| 3 | The chosen faction courts/tests them; a rival emerges (Tallytooth Ren, Veska Dunn, or Cole Ashby); the Compact watches if the player is bold. |
| 4 | Factions racing for the source treat the player as a competitor; Coalmont leans on them (or Reke quietly does, DM-Only); the Mourners/Wardens weigh whether to trust them with more. |
| 4→5 | The basin-scheme players (Compact, Ledger, and — hidden — the Court) register the player as a *factor* in the basin's fate; Act 2 factions begin to move. |

## DM Milestone Checklist (per level-up)

- Did the player achieve *any one* qualifying path? Level them — don't hold out for a specific quest.
- Pair the level-up with rest/downtime so the player can manage new resources (`../01_runner_protocol/DOWNTIME_PROTOCOL.md`).
- Lean to the *faster* end of pacing for a solo PC (`LEVELING_ASSUMPTIONS.md`); avoid flat stretches at L1–4 where survivability is thin.
- After any near-death or major R1 discovery, check whether a milestone is appropriate.
- Update `PLAYER_CHARACTER.md` (level, resources), `CURRENT_STATE.md`, and note the trigger in `SESSION_RECAP.md`.
- Keep the reveal cap at R1 through the whole act, including the Act 2 hand-off.
