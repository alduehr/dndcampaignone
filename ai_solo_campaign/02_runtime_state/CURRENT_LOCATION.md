## First Impression (player-safe)

Hollowmere rings a wide hollow of dark, still water. Stilt-houses and peat-smoke crowd the firm ground; planked walkways cross the boggy edges; and out in the flooded basin, when the fog thins, broken grey shapes show under the surface. The town smells of peat, salt, and cold water. People are kind but watchful, and every doorway has salt at the sill and a small star carved above it.

## Key Sub-Locations in Hollowmere

- **Mourner's Green** — cemetery and Wend's cottage; site of the opening failed rite.
- **The Drowned Lantern** — the inn; home base; rumor hub; Halla Coalmont keeps it, Sefra works the room.
- **The Boards** — stilt-house district; the Brevin cottage (Wren & Tomas).
- **The Rim** — market, council hall (Reeve Coalmont, Councilor Reke).
- **The Counting-House** — the Cinder Ledger's seat (Factor Domic Sael).
- **The Warden Rest** — modest Warden safe-house (Othetha when present; Hale).
- **The Basin Shore** — the flooded ruin's eerie edge; Old Sashe passes through.
