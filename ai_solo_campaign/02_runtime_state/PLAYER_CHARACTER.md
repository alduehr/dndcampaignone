# PLAYER_CHARACTER.md

## Purpose

Full record of the player character's current state. Updated whenever stats, equipment, resources, or conditions change. The AI DM loads this at session start to know the player's capabilities and current condition.

## Current Status

**Scaffold ready — awaiting character creation (Stage 2).** The campaign is built for **one player character starting at level 1** (any class/subclass/background works; the opening is class-agnostic). Milestone leveling per `../03_canon/LEVELING_ASSUMPTIONS.md` — no XP tracking needed. The AI DM fills this in during Session 1 setup (see `../16_ai_session_packs/START_NEW_CAMPAIGN_PROMPT.md` STEP 3). Do not invent the character; ask the player, or offer to generate a simple level-1 PC and confirm.

### Opening Condition Notes

- At campaign start, assume the PC is **uninjured**: full HP, no conditions, exhaustion 0, all resources/spell slots available.
- Starting equipment: per the chosen class/background (standard 5e/2024 starting gear). Record below once set.
- The PC is a **newcomer to Hollowmere**, arriving in Greyfall (late autumn), AQ 101, "for their own reasons" — let the player define these (work, a fresh start, a name to leave behind) and record them in Notes; weave them into the opening.
- The PC begins with the solo-play safety net available (safe base, optional companion, fen-guide, paid-work patron) — see `../16_ai_session_packs/SOLO_SAFETY_START.md`. None are companions *yet*; they are earned in play.
