# WORLD_CLOCKS.md

## Purpose

All active escalating threats, faction timelines, and world-event countdowns. Clocks advance when time passes, when the player ignores threats, or when specific triggers occur. The AI DM uses this to make the world feel alive and consequential.

```md
### [Clock Name]

- **Type:** faction | world event | disaster | political | personal | dungeon
- **Faction / NPC driving it:** 
- **Current stage:** 1 of 4 (or similar)
- **What triggers advancement:**
- **Stage 1:** [description — consequences begin]
- **Stage 2:** [description — pressure increases]
- **Stage 3:** [description — serious consequences]
- **Stage 4 / Final:** [description — point of no return or catastrophe]
- **How player can slow, stop, redirect, or exploit it:**
- **Last advanced:** [session number]
- **Clock file:** [path to faction file where clock is defined]
```
