# OPEN_THREADS.md

## Purpose

All unresolved loose ends — leads, unanswered questions, dangling hooks, unkept promises, unfollowed clues, and unresolved NPC situations. These are not full quests but are things the AI DM should remember and surface when appropriate.

## Current Status

**Seeded — campaign start (Stage 2).** Opening loose ends drawn from the opening scene, hooks, and rumors (`../16_ai_session_packs/OPENING_SCENES.md`, `../09_quests/HOOKS_TABLE.md`, `../09_quests/RUMORS_TABLE.md`). Surface these through fiction when fitting. Most "what the player does not know" notes are DM-only — keep them hidden.

## Open Threads

### The Failing Death-Rites

- **Type:** unanswered question (the world's central mystery, surface level)
- **Origin:** the opening failed funeral; the town's frightened mood
- **What the player knows:** Across the Reach, death-rites have been failing — the dead don't always rest. It is getting worse, and now it has happened to one of Hollowmere's own (Wren).
- **What the player does not know (DM):** The cause is the harvest restarting beneath the basin (M5/M1). Publicly, the cause is genuinely unknown — a real mystery, not a withheld fact.
- **How it could resolve:** Following Hook 1 and the clue paths in `../11_mysteries_and_secrets/MYSTERY_WEB.md`.
- **Urgency:** long-term (drives the whole campaign)
- **Related:** Hook 1, Hook 9; Wardens, Mourners' Circle

### Why Sefra Wants the Player on This

- **Type:** unresolved NPC / lead
- **Origin:** Sefra's pointed offer at the funeral ("there's coin in being capable")
- **What the player knows:** A fixer named Sefra is unusually keen to put the newcomer onto the Brevin matter.
- **What the player does not know (DM):** Sefra was paid by a Hollow Court cutout (via Reke) to steer a capable newcomer toward the Brevins and the shrine; she doesn't know whom she serves and is uneasy (M8, Secret 16). The steering thread.
- **How it could resolve:** Pressing Sefra, following the money, protecting her (Clock 8, The Steering Unravels).
- **Urgency:** short-term to long-term
- **Related:** Sefra Quick; M8

### The Basin-Drainage Scheme

- **Type:** dangling hook / political lead
- **Origin:** town tension; Councilor Reke's "progress"; the Mourners' fury
- **What the player knows:** Councilor Reke and the Cinder Ledger are pushing to drain the basin "for resettlement / water rights"; the Mourners' Circle is furious; Reeve Coalmont is uneasy.
- **What the player does not know (DM):** The scheme is secretly an effort to expose and reopen the drowned shrine; Reke is a knowing Hollow Court agent (Secret 9, M4; Clock 2).
- **How it could resolve:** Hooks 4 and 5; investigating Reke; swaying the Compact.
- **Urgency:** short-term (clock is live)
- **Related:** Reke, Coalmont; Reachward Compact, Cinder Ledger

### The Broken Grey Shapes in the Basin

- **Type:** unfollowed observation / foreshadowing
- **Origin:** first impression of Hollowmere — old structures visible under the flooded basin when the fog thins
- **What the player knows:** Something old and enormous lies drowned under the basin.
- **What the player does not know (DM):** It is the drowned shrine, the harvest's keystone, with the Hollow Court beneath (M2). Atmosphere now; a clue later — do not explain.
- **How it could resolve:** Hooks 4 and 6; Old Sashe's drift-map; the basin shore.
- **Urgency:** long-term
- **Related:** Sashe; the drowned shrine

### The Dead Walking the Wrong Way

- **Type:** lead / rumor (the uncanny)
- **Origin:** fen rumor; vivid in Opener C (figures in the bog facing the water)
- **What the player knows:** People say the restless dead in the fens are drifting *toward the water*.
- **What the player does not know (DM):** They are leaked Remembrances drawn to the shrine (M5, Secret 20); Old Sashe has mapped the drift.
- **How it could resolve:** Hook 6 with Old Sashe.
- **Urgency:** short-term to long-term
- **Related:** Old Sashe; the Greyfens
