## AI Use

**DM-ONLY. Do not surface, summarize, or hint at any entry below to the player.** This ledger answers, in one place: *what does each NPC hide, how secret is it, what gates its reveal, and which mystery (M-code) does it feed?* Use it to (a) avoid leaking secrets, (b) decide whether an NPC *can* be made to talk, and (c) route clues without contradicting the mystery web.

### Secrecy tiers (how guarded a secret is)
- **PLAYER-SAFE COMPLICATION** — a worry/grudge the NPC may volunteer; not a true secret. Safe to roleplay openly.
- **DISCOVERABLE** — earned with reasonable social/investigation effort; no hard gate. Low spoiler risk.
- **CLUE-GATED** — needs a specific lever (trust, leverage, an item, a prior clue, protection from a threat).
- **CAMPAIGN-MYSTERY-TIED** — touches the apex truth (the harvest / Quietfall / Hollow Court). Reveal only per `REVELATION_MAP.md`; never early.
- **LOCAL-COLOR** — a small private secret with no campaign weight (a cheat, an affair, a debt). Flavor only.

> M-codes (M1–M10) reference the campaign mystery chains in `MYSTERY_WEB.md`. Court = Hollow Court apex thread.

## Cross-Cutting Secret Clusters (DM map)

- **The "seal-keeper" Wardens** (Othetha, Adwen, Verge, Pell-adjacent, Sef, Ošric): each holds a *fragment* of the same buried purpose. Together they triangulate M6 without any one of them knowing the whole. Never let a low-tier Warden state the apex truth.
- **The Mourner "old-songs" network** (Wend, Combe, both Sennets, Bryd, Mam Tace, Mabb Tay, Ansel): carry the *folk* version of "the Concord ate the dead." This is the moral/M6 path, distinct from the Remnant's documentary path.
- **The Ledger relic-chain** (Vyre → Vell/Domic/Riis → Marlow, W.Pell, Cole, Coyle, Bly, Gull, Sorrel, Quint): every link knows a *piece* of M3; none but Vyre/Vell sees the whole monopoly design.
- **The cover-up officials** (Tindle, Frost, Ysarra, Cray, Falk, Quayle): institutional denial, not conspiracy. Each is a conscience-lever, not a villain.
- **The unwitting steerers** (Sefra, and any Court cutout): connect to Reke → the Court without knowing it. Protect this thread's reveal timing.
