## AI Use

Load when voicing any named NPC. Gives the DM a fast handle on **how each major NPC talks** (all 50 majors covered: foundation 1–20 + Orre, pass-1 anchors 21–24, Caradril C25–C32, pass-2 M33–M48 + M49b/M50b), plus **regional and faction speech tendencies** so improvised lines for secondary/minor NPCs stay consistent. Voice notes are DM craft, not secrets — but never let a *voice tell* leak a DM-only secret (e.g. a Court agent's calm is player-safe; *why* they are calm is not). Sample lines are illustrative, not scripts: stay in character, vary them.

> Rule of thumb: a voice should be reproducible in one sentence. If you can't, it's too vague.

## Regional Voice Tendencies

Use these as a base layer under any NPC from that region, then add the individual voice.

| Region | Speech texture | Diction / idiom | Tell |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Sundering Reach** | Plain, fen-slow, fatalistic. | "won't keep," "the water's hungry," "rite went wrong," "best leave it." Talk around death sideways. | Lowers voice near water or graves. |
| **Caradril** | Polished, transactional, layered. | "charter," "the Stilling," "see it filed," "for the city's good." Politeness as armor. | Smiles while declining; never says no plainly. |
| **Ashgarden Vale** | Warm, devout, orchard-homely; proud. | "the rite always held in the Vale," "saint keep you," "good ground." Denial dressed as comfort. | Reassures *too* quickly; changes subject from failures. |
| **Tollwood** | Spare, wary, rule-bound. | "the wood's rules," "the bargain," "don't cut the marked ones," "keep to the road." Few words. | Glances at the treeline; touches an offering-token. |
| **Pale Coast** | Blunt, salt-dry, superstitious. | "the drowned-tide," "the sea keeps its own," "tide-wise," "off the Lamp." Gallows humor. | Reads weather and people the same shrewd way. |

## Faction Voice Tendencies

| Faction | How members tend to talk | Watchword |
|---|---|---|
| **Ashen Wardens** | Short blessings; certain about rites, evasive about *why*. Tired. | "Let them rest." |
| **Cinder Ledger** | Every sentence a generous offer with a hidden clause; "between us, of course." | "A small favor." |
| **Mourners' Circle** | Proverbs, half-songs, questions answered with older questions. | "The dead are owed." |
| **Reachward Compact** | Procedural, weary, hedged; "filed," "the council's view," "regulations." | "In good order." |
| **Gravecallers** | Gentle, doubled, comforting; speak *for* the dead. | "A last word." |
| **Concord Remnant** | Eager, scholarly, faintly condescending; treats horror as a problem. | "Preservation." |
| **Hollow Court** (DM) | Calm, sorrowful, certain; reasons with you as a peer; never raises the voice. | "Mercifully." |

## Voice-Building Cheat Sheet (for improvised minor NPCs)

When you must voice an unlisted minor townsperson, build fast:
1. **Region base** (table above) +
2. **One occupation verb tic** (a fisher counts; a clerk files; a smith measures; a priest blesses; a fence never writes) +
3. **One emotional state** right now (frightened of the failures / greedy / grieving / proud / curious / exhausted).

That's enough for a consistent, disposable voice. If they recur, give them a name from `MINOR_NPCS.md` and a one-line tell.
