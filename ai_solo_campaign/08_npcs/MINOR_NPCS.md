## Usage Notes

- These NPCs are **disposable but consistent**: reuse the same name/hook if the player returns, but they need no growth unless promoted.
- Several carry a small thread to a major mystery (M5 grave-drift, the Gravecaller cells, the salvage trade, the thinning) — surface as *flavor and breadcrumb*, never as the apex truth.
- If a minor NPC becomes important in play, promote them to a SECONDARY_NPCS entry and add full mechanics.
