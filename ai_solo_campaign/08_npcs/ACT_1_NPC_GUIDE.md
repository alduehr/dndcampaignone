## AI Use

**Mixed secrecy — DM-facing.** A practical Act 1 casting guide. It **creates no new NPCs** — it points to existing entries in `MAJOR_NPCS.md` and `SECONDARY_NPCS.md` and explains each one's *Act 1 role*, *how the player meets them*, *what they want / offer / withhold*, and *what changes if the player builds or breaks the relationship*. Full profiles, voices, and DM-Only secrets live in the source files — load those for the secret. **Never surface a DM-Only secret here or there before it is earned; Act 1 caps reveals at R1** (`../11_mysteries_and_secrets/REVELATION_MAP.md`). The "Safe Re-Entry Points" section is the antidote to a stuck or lost player.

> **On the Hollow Court:** Reke appears below only as a *reasonable politician with a project* (a complication/patron). The Court itself never appears in Act 1, and there is **no friendly Hollow Court faction quest** — that is correct, not a gap. Reke's errand runs as Hook 4 ("The Councilor's Errand"), steering the player toward the basin without ever exposing the Court.

## Safe Re-Entry Points (for a lost, stuck, or stranded player)

If the player is adrift, send one of these *to them* — each restarts momentum without railroading and without needing any specific prior NPC.

| Re-Entry NPC | Why they're safe to fall back on | What they restart |
|---|---|---|
| **Halla Coalmont** (the Drowned Lantern) | Always at the hub; loves to talk; an early-warning system | Rumors, introductions, a base — the cheapest way to re-seed any hook |
| **Brother Hale** (roams with the Wardens) | Travels the Reach; always needs help with the next failing rite | The Warden door, R1 via the dead, a companion at the player's side |
| **Old Sashe** (Greyfens edge / basin shore) | The fens' guide; appears wherever the dead drift | The wilderness route to R1 (the drift), safe passage, the shrine's shape |
| **Hospitaller Sirrin** (the Ashwalk Rest) | Central crossroads; heals and resupplies anyone | A safe harbor, resupply, and a fresh Warden hook from anywhere central |
| **Reeve Coalmont** (Hollowmere hall) | Honest, fair, always needs trustworthy hands | The lawful/political door and the honest-Compact alliance |
| **Sefra Quick** (the Drowned Lantern) | The fixer who always has a job | A new paid lead (DM: she keeps nudging toward the basin — a hook, never a leash) |

**Re-entry rule:** if the player loses *every* tracked thread, default to Halla (a rumor), Hale (a failing rite), or Sashe (the drift). All three deliver R1 routes and an ally in one scene.

## DM Casting Notes

- **Don't over-deploy secrets.** Most Act 1 scenes use these NPCs' *player-safe* faces. Their DM-Only secrets (in the source files) are Act 2–4 fuel; cap Act 1 reveals at R1.
- **Patrons are interchangeable for content.** If a patron dies or is alienated (`ACT_1_FAILURE_STATES.md` Case 2), the *quest and clues* survive via the backup giver listed there and above.
- **Rivals recur — don't kill them cheaply.** Ashby, Ren, and Dunn are meant to return; let them flee, negotiate, or be talked down (`ACT_1_THREATS.md`).
- **Update memory.** After any meaningful interaction, update `../02_runtime_state/NPC_MEMORY.md` and `RELATIONSHIPS.md` (attitude, promises, debts, threats).
