## AI Use

Stage 9 secondary-NPC expansion — recurring local characters who fill **services, authority, local problems, faction contacts, hooks, and color** across every authored settlement, so the AI DM rarely invents a recurring named person. Each entry carries the secondary fields in compact form: **Role · First Impression · Motivation · Secret/Complication (DM) · Link · Knows · Enters Play · Combat**. **First Impression/Role/Motivation are player-safe; Secret/Complication is DM-only.** Combat Relevance gives a fallback profile per `DND_MECHANICS_REQUIREMENTS.md` Tier 2. These supplement (do not replace) the Stage 3/4/7 secondaries in `../SECONDARY_NPCS.md`.
