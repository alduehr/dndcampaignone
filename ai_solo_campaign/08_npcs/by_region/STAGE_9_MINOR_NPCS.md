- These NPCs are **disposable but consistent**: reuse name/hook on return; no growth unless promoted to secondary.

- The travelers/refugees pool is for road encounters and to seed cross-region rumor; reuse them anywhere appropriate.
- "(no kin)" / "*(kin to X)*" tags prevent accidental family-line invention. Shared surnames (Pell, Wend, Sael, Coalmont, Vane, Dree, Tull, Strand, Brunt, Gethin) are common frontier names — keep epithets.
