## AI Use

Locate quests appropriate to the PC's current level. Tiers per `DND_MECHANICS_REQUIREMENTS.md`. Far-continent developed quests (Stage 10) are level-banded by region; home-region quests (Reach/Caradril/Ring 1) and faction chains cluster in Tiers 1–2. Most quests scale ±2 levels (see each quest's Scaling Notes).

---

## Tier 1 — Levels 1–4 (Local danger, first mysteries)
- **Home regions:** all Act 1 quests (`act_1_quests/`); Sundering Reach regional (`regional_quests/` Reach files, Lvl 1–5); Ring 1 regional (Vale/Tollwood/Coast, Lvl 2–6); faction-chain intros/trust quests (Q?1–Q?2).
- **Caradril:** early city quests (Lvl 2–4).
- **Major:** the early Q_MAJOR spine quests (see `MAJOR_CAMPAIGN_QUESTS.md`).

## Tier 2 — Levels 5–8 (Regional influence, escalation)
- **Home regions:** later faction-chain quests (Q?3–Q?4, Lvl 4–6); higher Caradril city quests (Lvl 4–6); Ring 1 upper quests (Lvl 5–6).
- **Far continent:** **Marrowdowns** Q_MD_001–005 (6–10); lower **Verdance** / **Glassmere** / **Hethewald** / **Sunmark** / **Wender** quests (8+).
- **Major:** mid Q_MAJOR spine.

## Tier 3 — Levels 9–12 (Major political & magical consequences)
- **Far continent:** **Verdance Reaches** Q_VR_001–007 (8–12); **Glassmere League** Q_GL_001–008 (8–13); **Hollow Gulf Ports** Q_HGP_001–008 (9–14); **Sallowmarch** Q_SP_001–007 (9–13); **Hethewald** Q_HFH_001–007 (8–13); **Karran Marches** Q_KM_001–007 (9–14); **Sunmark** Q_SUN_001–007 (8–13); **Wender Steppe** Q_WS_001–005 (8–13); lower **Emberfell** Q_ET (10–15); lower **Saltmere** Q_SALT (11–15).
- **Major:** Q_MAJOR_011/012 (Remnant chain) begins to reach inland.

## Tier 4 — Levels 13–16 (World-scale threats, ancient powers)
- **Far continent:** **Concord Heartlands / Ruin'd Crown** Q_CH_001–006 (13–17); upper **Saltmere** Q_SALT (to 15); upper **Emberfell** Q_ET (to 15); upper **Karran** Q_KM (to 14); upper **Hollow Gulf** Q_HGP (to 14).
- **Major:** Q_MAJOR climax-approach; Hollow Court DM chain (QH3–QH4).

## Tier 5 — Levels 17–20 (Endgame, legendary threats, final truths)
- **Far continent:** top of **Concord Heartlands** Q_CH_002/003 (to 17) as the player approaches the great ruin.
- **Major:** the campaign-climax Q_MAJOR quests and the endgame (the apex truth resolves at Hollowmere, NOT the far continent).
