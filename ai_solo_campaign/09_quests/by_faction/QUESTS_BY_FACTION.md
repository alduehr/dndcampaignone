## AI Use

Locate quests tied to each of the seven major factions, across the whole continent. The seven majors are the ONLY major factions (per NAMING_REGISTRY); far-continent quests use these same factions plus light independent/regional powers and the placeholder Emberfell theocracy (a region with a faith, not an eighth major faction). Far-continent faction presence is an *arm* or *edge* of the home faction unless noted.

---

## Ashen Wardens
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/ASHEN_WARDENS/` (QW1–QW4).
- **Far continent:** the Wardens are a Reach-rooted order; far-region "seal-keeper"/anchorite appearances are oblique and rare (no dedicated far-continent Warden quests — the order's reach is the home regions and Ring 1).

## Cinder Ledger
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/CINDER_LEDGER/` (QL1–QL4); city `Q_THE_QUAY_CHARTER.md`, `Q_QUIET_COIN.md`.
- **Major:** Q_MAJOR_004 (the continental Ledger / monopoly), Q_MAJOR_018 (river of coin).
- **Far continent (anti-Ledger / Ledger-arm quests):** Q_VR_003 (Factor-General Crane), Q_VR_002 (debt-vise); Q_GL_002 (Holt–Ledger); Q_HGP_007 (sea-relic monopoly); Q_ET_005 (black-market relics); Q_SP_007 (delta exploitation); Q_HFH_005 (timber); Q_SALT_003 (the Ledger's salt); Q_KM_004 (the humming iron); Q_SUN_001/005 (Ledger-funded cutting / fair trade); Q_MD_003 (the wool and the Ledger); Q_WS_005 (Spine-Foot squeeze).

## Mourners' Circle
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/MOURNERS_CIRCLE/` (QM1–QM4); city `Q_THE_FUNERAL_THAT_WOULDNT_TAKE.md`.
- **Far continent (old-songs network / grief-keeping):** Q_VR_004 (fresh inland failures); Q_GL_005 (Lowwater); Q_HGP_004 (Mardenmouth sea-Mourners); Q_SP_004 (Mother Reed); Q_HFH_002 (Onn Greenward); Q_SALT_004 (the going-down song); Q_KM_006 ("Grey" Tess, oblique); Q_MD_004 (the old songs of the downs); Q_CH_006 (Onn Crale, near-source); cross-region JOB_XR_001, H_XR_002, R_XR_002.

## Reachward Compact
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/REACHWARD_COMPACT/` (QC1–QC4); city `Q_THE_SEALED_LETTER.md`.
- **Far continent:** the Compact is a Reach-government body; far regions have their own independent governments (the League, the Gulf ports, the Protectorate's distant crown, the steppe confederacy, the shire-moots, the warlord holds) — no dedicated far-continent Compact quests.

## Gravecallers
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/GRAVECALLERS/` (QG1–QG4); Reach/Coast cult-seeds.
- **Far continent (cult-spread / anti-cult):** Q_SALT_006 (the Listener at the Water); Q_KM_006 (the Deep Listener); Q_CH_004 (Father Ord / the Gravecaller mirror); cross-region JOB_XR_008, H_XR_007.

## Concord Remnant
- **Home chain:** `faction_quests/CONCORD_REMNANT/` (QR1–QR4); city `Q_THE_LANTERN_AND_THE_LAMP.md`, `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md`.
- **Major:** Q_MAJOR_011/012 (the Remnant chain to the Crown).
- **Far continent (Remnant field-work / the chain inland):** Q_VR_006 (Hadwin Vael); Q_GL_004 (the Reliquary); Q_ET_004 (Emberfell–Crown race, rival); Q_HFH_004 (the Old Holds); Q_KM_002 (the Old Iron forts / Wenna Stone, turnable); Q_SUN_006 (the reclaimed ruin); Q_MD_002 (Wenn Penmark, oblique); **Q_CH_002/003 (Hollin Vane, the Crown — apex-adjacent, the chain's end).**

## Hollow Court — DM-ONLY
- **DM chain:** `faction_quests/HOLLOW_COURT/` (QH1–QH4) — never surfaced to the player.
- **Far continent:** the Court NEVER appears in far-region player-facing content. Far-region "echoes" (the Saltmere going-down, the Crown's vanished inner circle, the Emberfell mirror) corroborate the theme but NEVER confirm the Court. Its seat is the Hollowmere keystone, not the far continent.

## Emberfell Theocracy (placeholder region-faith, NOT an eighth major faction)
- Q_ET_001–008 (`by_region/EMBERFELL_THEOCRACY_QUESTS.md`); rival presence at the Crown (Q_CH_005, Vurr Cindra). A thematic mirror, kept distinct from the keystone.

## Independent / Regional Powers (far continent)
- Salt-clans (Saltmere), warlord holds (Karran), grove-tribes (Sunmark), steppe confederacy (Wender), shire-gentry (Marrowdowns), free-holds (Hethewald), port city-states (Hollow Gulf), the Glassmere League, the Sallowmarch's distant crown, Crownmouth's free militia, and assorted outlaws ("Eel"/"Heron"/"Greenfinger"/"Crow" Maddox, Borr, etc.) — each carries its own quests in the relevant `by_region/` file.
