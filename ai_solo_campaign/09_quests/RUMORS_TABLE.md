## Rumor Use Notes

- Rumors 1–5 and 16 are loud and easy (Act 1 surface).
- Rumors 13 and 20 brush the deep truth but are dismissed as legend/superstition — **do not confirm them**; let them sit as eerie foreshadowing.
- Rumors 6, 9, 11, 15 are "follow-the-thread" leads into specific hooks/secrets.
- False/misleading rumors (4) are *fair*: they can be disproven through play (Hook 5).
