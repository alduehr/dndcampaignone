## AI Use
The trust-building link. The Remnant brings the player to Caradril and the Lantern Reach, deepening their Concord Script and tasking them with recovering or translating a significant text — earning entry toward the Sealed Archive. Delivers C-M6/M9-adjacent (the Concord's true scale and the deliberate Quietfall, oblique). First brush with the inner circle's secrecy (Wessel Dree, the Reclaimer gatekeeper). Reveals capped at R3-R4.

## Quest Type
Faction (Concord Remnant) — trust-building task

## Level Range
5-8 (scales). **Solo danger:** moderate. City intrigue, an archive's wards, a rival, optional Reclaimer pressure — telegraphed; investigation/social-primary.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Translate a difficult Concord text: Intelligence (Concord Script) DC 15.
- Gain entry to the Lantern Reach's restricted stacks: Persuasion/Deception DC 15.
- Notice the inner circle keeps certain texts off-limits: Insight/Investigation DC 16.
- Recover a contested text from a ruin or rival: varies; DC 14-16.

## Starting Hook
Briss, thrilled with the player's progress, brings them to Caradril's Lantern Reach: "You read better than half my colleagues now. There's a text — fragmentary, dangerous, contested — that could change everything we know about the Quietfall. Help me recover and translate it, and you'll have the run of the college." But the keeper of the deepest stacks, Wessel Dree, watches the player with cold, careful eyes.

## Location
Caradril — the Lantern Reach (the Lamplighters' Hall college; the edge of the Sealed Archive). A Reach ruin if the text must first be recovered.

## Involved NPCs
Lector Briss (sponsor; sincere), Sub-Lector Tamsin Orr (Script tutor; idealist; an ally), Keeper-Archivist Wessel Dree (Sealed Archive gatekeeper; a Reclaimer; cold and watchful), Veska Dunn (Reach field-agent, if the text is recovered from a ruin), optionally Archivist Quorrin Vane (a brief, evaluating appearance).

## Involved Factions
Concord Remnant (primary). Cinder Ledger (the Vyre-Quorrin deal looms in the background; C1). Salt Syndicate / the Hush (Caradril underworld, if the text is contested).

## Surface Problem
Recover and translate a significant Concord text for the Remnant — and earn standing in the college. The complication: the deepest stacks are guarded, and not by idealists.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
The text corroborates the Concord's **true scale and the deliberate Quietfall** (C-M6/M9-adjacent, oblique) — a major deep-history step toward R4. Wessel Dree is a **Reclaimer** quietly steering the player away from the most dangerous truths in the Sealed Archive; his watchfulness is the player's first concrete sign that the Remnant has an inner circle with secrets (seeds QR3). **The Hollow Court is never named; the player learns the Concord's history, not the Court's existence. Cap at R3-R4.**

## Objectives
1. Recover and/or translate the contested text.
2. Earn standing in the Lantern Reach college.
3. Notice the inner circle's secrecy (Dree's gatekeeping).

## Possible Approaches
- **Scholarship:** translate the text with growing Script mastery (Tamsin Orr assists).
- **Investigation:** recover the text from a ruin or rival; notice what Dree keeps hidden.
- **Social:** win the college's trust; charm or read Dree.
- **Stealth:** glimpse the restricted stacks Dree guards (a seed for `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md`).

## Obstacles
The text's difficulty; Dree's cold gatekeeping; a rival (Ledger or underworld); the dawning sense that the Remnant's leadership hides something; the Archive's wards.

## Important Scenes
- The Lantern Reach: lamplit stacks, the murmur of scholars, the locked door of the Sealed Archive.
- Translating the text and reading, in the player's own voice, that the Concord was *vaster and stranger* than the world remembers.
- Dree, intercepting the player near the restricted stacks with a smile that doesn't reach his eyes.

## Clues
- **C-M6/M9-adjacent (oblique):** the Concord's true scale and a hint of the deliberate Quietfall (R4-ward; no Court name).
- **QR3 seed:** the Remnant has an inner circle that hides certain truths (Dree's gatekeeping).

## Combat Options
Optional and avoidable: a rival crew or Syndicate toughs over the text (**Spy/Scout/Thug-like, AC 13, ~22-30 HP**); the Archive's magical wards (a hazard, not a fight). Telegraphed; the quest is winnable through scholarship and stealth. See `../../city_quests/Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md` and `../../../13_encounters_and_bestiary/CARADRIL_ENCOUNTERS.md`.

## Noncombat Options
Translation, investigation, persuasion, and stealth resolve the quest. Combat is avoidable.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
The danger is intrigue and wards, not damage; Briss and Tamsin Orr back the player; Dree obstructs but does not (yet) attack; the player can withdraw with the text or the knowledge gained; the Archive itself is a later, gated objective.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest in Caradril. Time pressure: the Vyre-Quorrin deal (C1) and the Reclamation clock (#7) advance in the background; the player's progress toward the Archive matters for Act 3.

## Rewards
- **Deeper Concord Script** and the run of the Lantern Reach college.
- C-M6/M9-adjacent deep-history (a major truth-step; R4-ward).
- Tamsin Orr as an idealist ally; the first concrete sign of the inner circle (seeds QR3).
- A foothold toward the Sealed Archive (`Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md`).

## Consequences
- **Succeed openly:** the player is a trusted scholar; the inner circle (Dree/Quorrin) begins watching them more closely (#7 noted).
- **Notice the secrecy:** the player gains the QR3 thread (the inner circle hides things).
- **Ignore:** the deep-history gate narrows (recoverable; M6/M9 also reachable via the Reach and the Mourner songs).
- **Cross the Reclaimers:** Dree and the inner circle grow hostile early (a harder QR3-QR4).

## Failure State
If the text is lost or the player alienates the college, the deep-history gate stays partly closed — reachable later via the Sealed Archive quest, the Reach ruins, or the Mourner songs (three-clue rule preserved). Not a block.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QR3 The Idealist and the Archivist; `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md` (the Archive proper); the Vyre-Quorrin deal (C1; ties to the Ledger chain); Dree's gatekeeping as the inner-circle thread.

## Scaling Notes
- **L5:** a single text, light gatekeeping.
- **L8:** a contested recovery, heavy Reclaimer scrutiny, the Archive's wards in play.
- Cap at R3-R4; never name the Court.
