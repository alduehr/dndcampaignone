## AI Use
The Concord Remnant faction quest chain (Stage 8). Carries a player drawn to the scholars from honest fieldwork (and the priceless gift of reading Concord Script) to the discovery of the inner circle's true aim — seizing the harvest to rebuild the Concord's empire. The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_REMNANT_THE_VISITING_SCHOLAR.md` is the on-ramp. Drives the Reclamation clock (#7) and the Deal (C1). The Remnant gates the deepest written history (M6/M9) — but the inner circle's secret is the chain's payload. The Hollow Court is never named (the Remnant are *rivals*, not allies, of the Court).

## Chain Throughline
The Remnant offers the one thing every truth-seeker needs: the ability to read Concord Script. Honest scholarship (Lector Briss) draws the player in — until they brush against the inner circle (Archivist Quorrin Vane) and the Reclaimers' real aim. The decision point splits the faction: expose Quorrin to the idealists and shatter the Reclaimers, or help them seize the harvest to rebuild the Concord — a new tyranny on the dead.

## Key NPCs
Lector Briss (idealist public face; clue source), Archivist Quorrin Vane (inner-circle leader; the secret), Sub-Lector Tamsin Orr (Script tutor; idealist), Keeper-Archivist Wessel Dree (Sealed Archive gatekeeper; Reclaimer), Veska Dunn (Reach field-agent).
