## AI Use
The Reachward Compact faction quest chain (Stage 8). Carries a player working for the frontier government from honest hire to the heart of its hidden infiltration — the basin scheme and the corrupt councilor steering it. This chain is the player's most direct handle on the **Basin Drains clock (#2)**, the master infrastructure clock that feeds the harvest's restart (#1). The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_COMPACT_THE_REEVES_DOUBT.md` is the on-ramp. Reke's true nature (Hollow Court agent) is gated; the player exposes him as a corrupt councilor first (Secret 9), the deeper truth far later.

## Chain Throughline
A capable newcomer is exactly what the under-staffed Compact needs — and exactly the instrument Councilor Reke wants to steer. Each task draws the player closer to the basin-drainage scheme until they hold proof it's built on doctored surveys and a corrupt councilor. The decision point is a council vote that can slow or accelerate the master clock racing the whole campaign.

## Key NPCs
Reeve Marda Coalmont (honest reeve; ally), Councilor Aldous Reke (the infiltrator), Clerk Pevin Oss (frightened records-keeper; the proof), Sergeant Hesk (watch leader), Bailiff Aldwin Cray (grey patron, Kettle Bridge).
