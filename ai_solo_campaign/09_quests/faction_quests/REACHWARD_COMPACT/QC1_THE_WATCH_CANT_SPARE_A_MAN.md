## AI Use
First link of the Compact chain. A simple deputized job (a job the thin watch can't handle) introduces the frontier government, earns honest pay, and lets both Coalmont (honest) and Reke (the infiltrator) take the player's measure. Income source. No deep reveal; the player simply becomes "the Compact's capable hand."

## Quest Type
Faction (Reachward Compact) — intro hook

## Level Range
1-3 (scales). **Solo danger:** low-to-moderate. Bandits, a fen predator, or a missing-person mystery — telegraphed, with social outs.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Deputized investigation/tracking: Investigation/Survival DC 13.
- Talk down or arrest a troublemaker: Persuasion/Intimidation DC 13.
- Read which councilor to trust: Insight DC 13 (Coalmont straight; Reke smooth).

## Starting Hook
Reeve Coalmont, overworked and short-handed, deputizes the player for a job the watch can't cover: a caravan menaced on the road, a body found in the fen, or a dispute turning ugly. "I can't spare a man and I can't ignore it. You look capable. The Compact pays honest coin for honest work."

## Location
Hollowmere council hall (the brief) and wherever the job leads (road, fen-edge, or a Reach village).

## Involved NPCs
Reeve Marda Coalmont (honest employer), Sergeant Hesk (watch leader; gruff partner), Councilor Aldous Reke (appears, charming, "impressed" by the newcomer — the steering begins), Clerk Pevin Oss (hands over paperwork; nervous).

## Involved Factions
Reachward Compact (primary). Whatever faction the job touches (bandits, a grieving family, a smuggler).

## Surface Problem
A frontier problem the watch is too thin to solve. Be the Compact's capable hand.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
Coalmont's offer is exactly what it seems. But **Reke notices the player** and begins, gently, to take an interest — the first thread of the steering toward the basin/shrine. Nothing about Reke's true nature surfaces; he is simply "a friendly senior councilor." (M8/Steering backdrop.)

## Objectives
1. Complete the deputized job (investigate, protect, arrest, or mediate).
2. Report to the Compact.
3. Make a first impression on Coalmont and Reke.

## Possible Approaches
- **Investigation:** solve the missing-person/menace.
- **Social:** mediate the dispute or talk down the troublemaker.
- **Protective/combat (fallback):** guard the caravan, fight off bandits (telegraphed, escapable).

## Obstacles
The under-resourced watch; an uncooperative witness; bandits or a fen predator; the first subtle pull of Reke's flattery.

## Important Scenes
- Coalmont's tired, honest brief.
- Reke "happening by" to praise the newcomer and plant a friendly seed.
- Reporting back — and the Compact marking the player as a reliable hire.

## Clues
- A character read: Coalmont is honest; Reke is *too* smooth (an Insight seed, not a flagged clue).
- Faint M8 backdrop (Reke takes an interest).

## Combat Options
Bandits or a fen predator — **Bandit/Beast-like, AC 12-13, ~11-22 HP**, telegraphed, escapable, morale-bound. See `../../../13_encounters_and_bestiary/SUNDERING_REACH_ENCOUNTERS.md`.

## Noncombat Options
Most jobs resolve by investigation and persuasion.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Hesk or a watchman can back the player; the job is sized for a solo PC; retreat and reporting-for-reinforcement are allowed.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Light; safe rest in Hollowmere. A delayed job lets the problem worsen (a complication, not a block).

## Rewards
- Compact coin (honest early income).
- Standing as the Compact's capable hand (the chain's gate).
- Coalmont's trust; Reke's "friendly" interest (the steering seed).

## Consequences
- **Success:** the Compact relies on the player for harder jobs (QC2 opens); Coalmont and Reke both court them.
- **Ignore:** the watch muddles through; the player simply isn't the Compact's go-to yet.
- **Excessive force:** a heavy-handed arrest or killing draws a watch review; Coalmont disapproves.

## Failure State
A botched job is a complication (the menace persists, a dispute festers) — the Compact is mildly disappointed, not closed off. QC2 still opens.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QC2 The Doctored Survey; `../../regional_quests/Q_THE_BAILIFFS_LADDER.md` (Compact politics); Reke's deepening interest (M8).

## Scaling Notes
- **L1:** a simple mediation or a single menace.
- **L3:** a layered investigation with a small fight.
