## AI Use
The Gravecallers' faction quest chain (Stage 8). Carries a player drawn to the outlawed cult from a furtive first contact to the richest truth-source in the early-mid campaign — and finally to the cult's internal split: patient learning vs. catastrophic shrine-breaking. The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_GRAVECALLER_A_LAST_WORD.md` is the on-ramp. Drives the Breaking clock (#5). The dead's own testimony is the chain's payload — the truest (and most dangerous) M5/M6 path. The Court is never named.

## Chain Throughline
A curious or grieving newcomer earns the trust of a persecuted cult that can genuinely speak with the dead. From the dead themselves, the player learns fragments of the world's deepest truth — and is pulled into the cult's fatal divide: the Tallow Man's patient learning versus Cole Ashby's drive to shatter a shrine now, whatever the cost. The player decides whether the Gravecallers become a careful truth-keeper or a catastrophe.

## Key NPCs
The Tallow Man (speaker; channels a Quietfall-era witness), Sister Knell (gentle recruiter), Cole Ashby (radical zealot), Iola Wend (the secret Mourner bridge).
