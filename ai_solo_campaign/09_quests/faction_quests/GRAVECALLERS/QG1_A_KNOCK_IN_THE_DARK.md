## AI Use
First link of the Gravecallers chain. A gentle recruiter (Sister Knell) makes furtive contact, offering the grieving "a last word" — and testing whether the player can be trusted with the cult's secret world. Introduces the Gravecaller Knock (signaling code) and the cult's persecuted reality. No deep reveal; the player learns the cult is real, hunted, and not simply evil.

## Quest Type
Faction (Gravecallers) — intro hook

## Level Range
2-4 (scales). **Solo danger:** low-to-moderate. Watch patrols, a wary cell, a fen route — telegraphed; social/stealth-primary.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Learn and use the Gravecaller Knock: Investigation/Insight DC 13.
- Convince a wary cell you're no informer: Persuasion/Insight DC 14.
- Evade a watch sweep en route: Stealth DC 13.
- Spot the cult's fen-trail markers: Perception/Survival DC 13.

## Starting Hook
A soft-spoken woman — Sister Knell — finds the player after a death or a hard day: "You've a kindness about you, and a question you haven't asked. The dead still speak, you know. If you ever wanted to *hear* one... knock twice, then once, at the old door past the reeds." It's an invitation, and a test, and a crime to accept.

## Location
Hollowmere's quiet corners and a Greyfens-edge cell (the Drowned Blind or a hidden ruin).

## Involved NPCs
Sister Knell (gentle recruiter; first contact), a grieving local she's also courting (a mirror for the player), an outlying cell member (wary, testing), optionally the watch (Sergeant Hesk's patrols).

## Involved Factions
Gravecallers (primary). Reachward Compact (outlaws them; the watch is a hazard). Mourners' Circle (despise the cult publicly).

## Surface Problem
A furtive invitation into the outlawed cult. Accept, refuse, or investigate — and decide what the Gravecallers really are.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
The Gravecallers genuinely speak with Remembrances and are persecuted, desperate, and partly right (M5/M6 source — not surfaced yet). Knell is sincere; she's looking for someone who hears the dead with compassion, not fear. This is a trust-test; passing opens the chain and the truest clue path in the early campaign. **No reveal beyond "the cult is real and not simply evil." Cap at R1-R2.**

## Objectives
1. Make contact (learn the Knock; reach the cell).
2. Prove you're not an informer.
3. Decide your stance (curious ally, cautious investigator, or opponent).

## Possible Approaches
- **Social:** earn the cell's trust by showing compassion for the dead.
- **Stealth:** reach the cell unseen by the watch.
- **Investigation:** verify the cult's claims (do the dead really answer?) before committing.
- **Opposition:** infiltrate to expose them (a Compact/Warden-aligned path — see Quests Against the faction).

## Obstacles
The watch's sweeps; the cell's justified paranoia; the social stigma (being seen near Gravecallers costs the player with the Mourners/Compact); the eerie strangeness of the cult's rites.

## Important Scenes
- Knell's quiet, kind invitation amid grief.
- The Knock at the reed-door; the long pause before it opens.
- A first, unsettling glimpse of the cult: ordinary grieving people, not monsters, doing a forbidden thing.

## Clues
- The Gravecaller Knock (signaling code; a reusable access tool).
- M5/M6 *seed*: the dead "answer" (the cult's claim, unverified) — points toward the chain's payload.

## Combat Options
Optional and avoidable: a watch patrol (**Guard-like, AC 16, ~22 HP**) or a spooked cell member. Telegraphed; the quest is winnable by stealth and talk. See `../../../13_encounters_and_bestiary/SUNDERING_REACH_ENCOUNTERS.md`.

## Noncombat Options
Contact, trust, and investigation resolve the quest. Combat is avoidable.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
The cult is wary, not murderous, of a compassionate newcomer; the watch can be evaded; the player can refuse the invitation with no penalty beyond a missed opportunity; the stigma is social, not lethal.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Light; safe rest in Hollowmere. The cult is patient; the watch's sweeps create occasional urgency.

## Rewards
- The Gravecaller Knock (access to cells Reach-wide).
- Sister Knell as a first contact (gate to QG2).
- A first, true sense of the cult (real, hunted, not evil).

## Consequences
- **Accept/earn trust:** the cell opens; QG2 follows; the player gains a forbidden but rich relationship (Compact/Mourner suspicion if seen).
- **Investigate cautiously:** the player verifies and can still choose; trust is provisional.
- **Refuse:** no penalty; the chain can reopen later via Knell or a grieving contact.
- **Infiltrate to expose:** a Compact/Warden-aligned path; the cult becomes an enemy (Quests Against).
- **Betray Knell:** the cult marks the player an enemy of the dead (telegraphed reprisals later).

## Failure State
If the player is exposed to the watch or rebuffed by the cell, contact is lost for now — recoverable through another grieving contact or a later act of compassion. Not a block.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QG2 The Tallow Man's Word; `Q_THE_SECOND_MARK.md` (a cult cell and its sacred site); the Mourners' Iola bridge (seeded for later).

## Scaling Notes
- **L2:** a simple contact and trust-test.
- **L4:** a watch sweep and a more guarded cell.
