## AI Use
The Mourners' Circle faction quest chain (Stage 8). Carries a player who earns the grief-keepers' trust from a single funeral to the old songs (a major clue path) and finally to the Circle's internal schism: patient grief vs. militant shrine-breaking. The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_MOURNER_SALT_AND_SONG.md` is the on-ramp. Drives the Circle-Turns-Militant clock (#4). The old songs gate toward M5/M6 (the harvest's moral truth) without naming the Court.

## Chain Throughline
A newcomer who treats the dead with respect earns the Circle's trust, then its deepest inheritance — the old songs that remember the Concord's sin. That knowledge forces a reckoning: the Circle is splitting between elders who counsel patient grief and radicals stockpiling tools to destroy a shrine. The player tips which way the Circle goes, and whether it allies with the catastrophic Gravecallers.

## Key NPCs
Grandmother Wend (eldest; the songs), Iola Wend (apprentice; the bridge to the Gravecallers), Bann Oester (grave-digger; muscle), Elder Mabb Tay (Greywater Holm elder), Mother Ezrith Combe (Vale Mourner elder).
