## AI Use
First link of the Warden chain. A missing Warden draws the player into the order's world and earns a first measure of trust. Delivers M5 corroboration (the failures cluster) without any deep reveal. Cap reveals at R1.

## Quest Type
Faction (Ashen Wardens) — intro hook

## Level Range
2-4 (scales). **Solo danger:** low-to-moderate. One or two telegraphed Remembrances; a fen hazard. Noncombat-primary.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Track the missing Warden's road/route: Survival DC 13.
- Read a half-finished rite at the site: Religion DC 13.
- Calm or lay a loose Remembrance: Religion DC 13 (with the recovered rite-tools, DC 11).
- Notice the failure-cluster pattern (M5): Investigation/Religion DC 13, cumulative.

## Starting Hook
At the Ashwalk Rest (or via Hale), word comes that a circuit-riding Warden has missed two waystations. His ash-grey cloak was found snagged on a fen-thorn near Reedford, empty. "He never leaves the road. Something kept him."

## Location
The Reedford ford, the central Concord road, and a fen-edge ruin where the Warden went to lay a failed rite alone.

## Involved NPCs
Warden Brother Hale (sends or accompanies the player), Hospitaller Warden Sirrin (Ashwalk Rest quartermaster; gives the lead and rite-salt), the missing Warden (found alive but shaken, or — if the player is slow — dead at the ruin).

## Involved Factions
Ashen Wardens (primary). Mourners' Circle (a grieving Reedford family whose rite he was answering).

## Surface Problem
A Warden vanished answering a failing rite. Find him; finish the rite he could not.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
The rite "won't take" because the harvest pulls at the dead near a leaking road-node (M5). The Warden was overwhelmed, not attacked — the dead held him in place, sorrowful, not violent. **No deeper reveal**; the player learns only that failures cluster and worsen toward the basin.

## Objectives
1. Trace the missing Warden's route from the empty cloak.
2. Reach the fen-edge ruin and find him (alive if prompt).
3. Complete the failed rite he could not finish.

## Possible Approaches
- **Tracking/Exploration:** follow the route, read sign, reach the ruin.
- **Ritual:** finish the rite (Hale or Sirrin teaches the steps).
- **Social:** the Reedford family's account points the way (Insight/Persuasion).
- **Protective:** shield the shaken Warden while completing the rite.

## Obstacles
Fen hazards (mire, cold, fog); one or two loose Remembrances drawn to the same node; the half-laid rite resisting completion.

## Important Scenes
- The empty cloak on the thorn; the road going quiet.
- The ruin: the Warden kneeling, surrounded by sorrowful Remembrances that will not leave, his voice gone hoarse from a rite that will not close.
- Completing the rite together as the dead finally go.

## Clues
- **C-M5 (cumulative):** the failure clusters near the road-node; the dead drift basin-ward.
- A Warden observation (seed, not secret): "The closer to the water, the harder they hold."

## Combat Options
1-2 Remembrances — **specter-like, AC 12, ~22-30 HP**, non-hostile by default, layable by rite. Combat only if one breaks loose; telegraphed. Hale assists if present (**Acolyte/Priest-like**). See `../../../13_encounters_and_bestiary/ACT_1_THREATS.md`.

## Noncombat Options
The rite and de-escalation resolve every beat. Combat is the fallback.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Sirrin's rite-salt and instruction; Hale as optional ally; the dead are sorrowful, not ambushers; retreat-and-fetch-help always allowed; the basin-adjacent danger is flagged before engagement.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest at the Ashwalk Rest or Reedford. Time pressure: if the player delays days, the missing Warden is found dead and the rite must be done over his body (a harder, grimmer version — not a campaign block).

## Rewards
- Warden trust (first step of the chain; eases clock #3 slightly).
- Rite-salt and a minor healing draught (level-appropriate).
- Hale's deepened loyalty; an invitation to the next task (QW2).
- Cumulative M5 insight.

## Consequences
- **Success:** the Warden is saved or laid with honor; the Wardens mark the player as reliable.
- **Partial:** the rite half-takes; the dead return later (a follow-up scene).
- **Ignore:** the Warden dies; the Wardens strain further (#3 advances); the chain can restart via Hale with cooler trust.
- **Nonviolent:** ideal; maximal Warden/Mourner goodwill.
- **Excessive force** (fighting the dead instead of laying them): the Wardens are disturbed; trust stalls — a redirect, not a block.

## Failure State
A botched/abandoned rite leaves a loose Remembrance and a frightened Reedford — a complication and an M5 data-point, not a dead end. The chain continues; the Wardens are simply warier.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QW2 Salt and Ash; `../../regional_quests/Q_PELLS_DOUBT.md` (a Warden questioning the order); the basin scheme (the cause).

## Scaling Notes
- **L2:** one weak Remembrance (~22 HP); Hale does most of the rite; soften DCs by 1.
- **L4:** two Remembrances (~30 HP) near a clearer node; the rite may need two successes.
- Never reveal the order's secret here; cap at R1.
