## AI Use

The Ashen Wardens' multi-tier faction quest chain (Stage 8). It carries a player aligned with the rite-keepers from first contact to a decision point that affects the Wardens-Break clock (#3) and the player's standing for the "seal it" endgame. The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_WARDEN_THE_TRUE_RITE.md` is the on-ramp; this chain extends it into Acts 2–3. Reveals stay gated — Othetha's true secret (Secret 8) is never given before its earned point.

## Chain Throughline

A newcomer who helps the Wardens becomes, step by step, trusted enough to glimpse the order's fracture: the young who want to fight openly versus the elders sworn to silence. The decision point asks the player to choose whose vision of the Wardens to back — and that choice shapes whether the order holds together to guard the seal or breaks.

## Key NPCs

Warden Brother Hale (companion), Warden Sister Othetha (secret-keeper, trust-gated), Warden Pell (the doubter), Hospitaller Warden Sirrin (the Ashwalk Rest quartermaster).
