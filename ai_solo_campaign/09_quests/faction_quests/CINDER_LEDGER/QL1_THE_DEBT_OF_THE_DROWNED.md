## AI Use
First link of the Ledger chain. A simple, well-paid collection job introduces the Ledger's reach and the player's first moral nudge: the debtor is a grieving family. How the player handles it sets the Ledger's read on them (asset, soft touch, or problem). Income source for a low-level solo PC. No deep reveal.

## Quest Type
Faction (Cinder Ledger) — intro hook

## Level Range
1-3 (scales). **Solo danger:** low. Social-primary; an optional scuffle with a desperate debtor or a rival collector.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Collect without cruelty (find a fair arrangement): Persuasion/Insight DC 12.
- Detect the debtor's true means (can they really pay?): Insight DC 13.
- Intimidate (the hard way): Intimidation DC 13 (with consequences).
- Spot a rival collector skimming the Ledger: Investigation DC 13.

## Starting Hook
Factor Domic Sael, pleasant and precise, has a job: a Reedford widow is three payments behind on a resettlement loan since her husband drowned in the fens. "Nothing unkind. Just... encourage her to remember her obligations. The Ledger always remembers." Good silver for an afternoon's walk.

## Location
Hollowmere counting-house (the brief) and Reedford (the debtor).

## Involved NPCs
Factor Domic Sael (the employer; reasonable, watchful), Tallytooth Ren (offered as "backup"; the menace), the widow debtor (grieving, nearly broke), optional rival collector skimming the route.

## Involved Factions
Cinder Ledger (primary). Mourners' Circle (the widow is theirs; cruelty here earns lasting Circle cold).

## Surface Problem
A debt to collect from a grieving widow. Simple — except for the morality of it.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
The Ledger is using cheap resettlement loans to bind the frontier; the widow's husband died near a leaking road-node (a faint M5 backdrop, not surfaced). Domic is testing the player's spine and conscience both — and quietly hopes they're decent (his own conscience thread, QL4 seed).

## Objectives
1. Collect, restructure, forgive, or refuse the debt.
2. Decide how much cruelty (if any) to use.
3. Report to Domic.

## Possible Approaches
- **Compassionate:** restructure the debt or pay it down yourself (Domic notes a "soft touch"; the Circle warms).
- **Procedural:** collect what's fair without menace (the professional default).
- **Hard:** intimidate or seize goods (fast, but the Circle turns cold and Domic marks the player as a blunt instrument).
- **Investigative:** expose a rival collector skimming the route (impresses Domic; earns a bonus).

## Obstacles
The widow's grief and poverty; Tallytooth Ren's looming presence; the temptation of the Ledger's coin; the Circle watching how outsiders treat their grieving.

## Important Scenes
- Domic's smiling brief, every sentence a contract.
- The widow's cottage: nothing to take but a dead man's tools.
- Reporting back — and Domic's quiet, telling reaction to *how* the player did it.

## Clues
- A faint M5 backdrop (the husband drowned near a road-node) — flavor, not a flagged clue.
- Domic's character tell (he's relieved if the player was kind) — seeds QL4.

## Combat Options
Optional only: a cornered desperate debtor or a rival collector — **Commoner/Thug-like, AC 11-13, ~9-22 HP**, never lethal-by-ambush, morale breaks fast. See `../../../13_encounters_and_bestiary/SUNDERING_REACH_ENCOUNTERS.md`.

## Noncombat Options
The whole job is social. Combat is an avoidable failure-branch.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
No real threat to the player; Ren is backup, not an enemy; the player can decline the job at the cost of a little Ledger goodwill.

## Rest And Time Pressure
No pressure; safe rest anywhere. The Ledger waits.

## Rewards
- Ledger coin (good early income; bonus for exposing the skimmer).
- Domic Sael's read on the player (asset / soft touch / blunt instrument) — shapes future Ledger offers.
- An opening to QL2.

## Consequences
- **Kind:** the Circle warms; Domic marks a "soft touch" (fewer brutal jobs, more delicate ones); QL4 conscience-path strengthened.
- **Procedural:** standard Ledger trust; the chain proceeds neutrally.
- **Hard:** fast coin; the Circle turns cold (#4 nudge); Domic uses the player for dirtier work later.
- **Refuse:** minor Ledger cool; the chain can resume via a different job.
- **Excessive force:** a hurt widow draws Mourner fury and a watch complaint — a redirect, not a block.

## Failure State
If the collection goes badly (violence, a fled debtor), the Ledger writes a small loss and the player's Ledger standing dips slightly. No block; QL2 still opens.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QL2 The Quiet Buyer; the Mourners' Circle chain (if the player was cruel, they remember); Domic's conscience thread toward QL4.

## Scaling Notes
- **L1:** trivial; emphasize the moral texture, not danger.
- **L3:** add the rival-skimmer subplot for a meatier job; small combat possible.
