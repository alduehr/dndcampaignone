## AI Use
The Cinder Ledger's faction quest chain (Stage 8). Carries a player who takes the Ledger's coin from a simple job to a decision point about whether to help the Ledger's monopoly close, break it, or hijack it. The **Act 1 alignment quest** `../../act_1_quests/Q_ACT1_LEDGER_A_QUIET_SALVAGE.md` is the on-ramp. The Vyre-Quorrin deal (city clock C1) and the Monopoly clock (#6) are what this chain drives or derails. Hidden agenda (sell immortality) is gated; the player learns it through play.

## Chain Throughline
The Ledger pays well and asks little — at first. Each task draws the player deeper into the relic trade until they hold proof of what the Ledger is really buying and why. The internal split (Domic Sael's conscience vs. Vyre's ambition) becomes the lever: the player can help the monopoly close, shatter it by exposing the deal, or turn the Ledger against its own matriarch.

## Key NPCs
Factor Domic Sael (local face; conscience), Mother Calla Vyre (matriarch; ambition), Tallytooth Ren (enforcer; turnable), Provost Anneth Vell (Caradril deputy), Underfactor Coll Riis (turnable clerk).
