## AI Use
The moral-complication link. The Ledger trusts the player enough to move its prize: the vault relic that "remembers" the harvest. The player learns what the Ledger really intends (sell immortality) and must decide whether to serve it, expose it, or steal the proof. Delivers C-M3/M6-adjacent (a relic that remembers the Concord's harvest). Reveals capped at R3-R4.

## Quest Type
Faction (Cinder Ledger) — moral complication

## Level Range
5-8 (scales). **Solo danger:** moderate. Vault guards, a Remnant intercept, or a Syndicate fence — all telegraphed; heavy noncombat options.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Move/secure the relic discreetly: Stealth/Deception DC 15.
- "Listen" to what the relic remembers: Arcana/Religion DC 15.
- Read Vyre's true intent from Domic's unease: Insight DC 15.
- Bypass vault security (if stealing it): Thieves' Tools/Investigation DC 15-17 (see `Q_THE_QUAY_CHARTER.md`).

## Starting Hook
Domic, uncharacteristically uneasy, gives the player a sealed crate and an order from Vyre herself: escort the Ledger's most valuable relic — one that "must never be appraised by outsiders" — to a private buyer (or to Caradril for the Vyre-Quorrin deal). "Don't open it. Don't ask. Just... be the one I can trust with it." He clearly wishes he could ask the same.

## Location
The Hollowmere counting-house vault and the road/route toward Caradril (or a private Reach buyer). Optionally the Ledger Keep (Caradril) for the hand-off.

## Involved NPCs
Domic Sael (conscience fraying), Mother Calla Vyre (the order; appears if the route reaches Caradril), Tallytooth Ren (escort/muscle; turnable), Provost Anneth Vell (Vyre's Caradril deputy), an intercepting Remnant agent or Salt Syndicate fence.

## Involved Factions
Cinder Ledger (primary). Concord Remnant (wants the same relic for the deal). Salt Syndicate (would fence it). Mourners' Circle (would call it the worst grave-robbery yet).

## Surface Problem
Move the Ledger's prize relic safely to its buyer. The complication: the player can open it, and what they'd learn changes everything.

## Hidden Truth (DM-Only)
The relic **"remembers" the Concord's harvest** — listening to it is a C-M3/M6-adjacent clue (the dead held inside it witnessed the harvest; it does *not* yet name the Custodians/Court). Vyre intends it as the centerpiece of the **Vyre-Quorrin monopoly deal** (city clock C1; #6/#7). Domic suspects the relic is monstrous and is silently begging the player to be better than the Ledger. **Reveals capped at R3-R4; the Court is never named.**

## Objectives
1. Decide whether to open/listen to the relic.
2. Deliver it, steal it, expose it, or destroy/hide it.
3. Manage Domic, Vyre, and the interceptors accordingly.

## Possible Approaches
- **Loyal delivery:** serve the Ledger; the deal advances (#6/C1); maximal Ledger trust, maximal moral cost.
- **Listen, then deliver:** gain the clue, still serve the Ledger (the "knowing accomplice" path).
- **Steal/expose:** take the relic as proof of what the Ledger is doing; bring it to the Wardens, Mourners, Remnant idealists, or the Compact — shatters the deal (#6/C1 setback), makes the Ledger an enemy.
- **Turn Domic:** use his conscience to get the truth and a witness against Vyre (seeds QL4).

## Obstacles
The sealed crate's temptation; vault/escort security; the Remnant or Syndicate intercept; Vyre's appraising attention; the dawning horror of what the relic is.

## Important Scenes
- The vault: the crate among the Ledger's hoard, humming faintly.
- Listening: a dead voice inside the relic, describing being "taken" — the player's first earwitness of the harvest's mechanism (R3-R4, unnamed).
- Domic, alone, almost confessing his fear: "I don't want to know what we're selling. But you might have to."
- (If reaching Caradril) Vyre receiving it with serene, terrifying satisfaction.

## Clues
- **C-M3/M6-adjacent:** the relic remembers the harvest (a dead witness; no Court name).
- **C1/#6:** the relic is the deal's centerpiece — confirming the Ledger-Remnant monopoly plot.

## Combat Options
Vault guards (**Guard/Veteran-like, AC 16, ~30-45 HP**), an interceptor (**Spy/Scout-like**), or Ren (turnable). All telegraphed; the quest is winnable with zero combat. See `../../../10_dungeons_and_ruins/THE_LEDGER_VAULT.md`.

## Noncombat Options
Stealth, deception, persuasion, and Domic's conscience can resolve every branch without a fight.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
The relic is a clue, not a trap; Ren can be turned to backup; the player can decline the job (Ledger cool, not hostile); every interceptor is escapable; stealing the relic can be done by guile, not force.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest before the run. Time pressure: the Vyre-Quorrin deal (C1) advances if the player delays delivery, but stealing/exposing the relic *stalls* it.

## Rewards
- **If loyal:** peak Ledger trust; coin; standing for the QL4 decision from inside the Ledger.
- **If listened:** the harvest clue (R3-R4) — a major mystery step.
- **If stolen/exposed:** physical proof of the Ledger's crime (a power-card against Vyre and the deal); new allies among the Ledger's enemies.
- Domic as a potential witness/ally (seeds QL4).

## Consequences
- **Loyal delivery:** the deal advances (#6/C1 toward Stage 2-3); the player is complicit; the "sell it" endgame strengthens.
- **Listen + deliver:** the player knows the truth and chose profit anyway — a defining grey moment.
- **Steal/expose:** #6/C1 setback; the Ledger becomes an enemy (surveillance, debt-calling, blacklisting — never cheap death); the Wardens/Mourners/Remnant-idealists gain a proof.
- **Turn Domic:** the strongest setup for QL4; Vyre grows suspicious.
- **Excessive force:** a bloodbath at the vault brings the watch and Vyre's full economic war.

## Failure State
If the run fails (relic lost, player caught), the relic falls to an interceptor (the Remnant, advancing #7, or the Syndicate) — a complication that reshapes the board, not a block. QL4 still resolves the chain.

## Follow-Up Hooks
QL4 The Factor's Conscience; the Vyre-Quorrin deal (C1; `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE.md`); the Remnant chain (if they took the relic); the proof as an endgame power-card.

## Scaling Notes
- **L5:** light security; one interceptor.
- **L8:** full vault security; a Remnant crew; Vyre personally attentive.
- Cap reveals at R3-R4; the Court is never named by the relic.
