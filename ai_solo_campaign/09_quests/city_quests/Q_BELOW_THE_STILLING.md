## AI Use

The city's **exploration/delve quest**: descend the Sunken Wards beneath Caradril — read the warming Concord Script (M2/M6 *fragments*), find a dormant relic (M3), or use the Wards back-way into the Sealed Archive. The city's graded dungeon, levels 6–10, retreat-rich. **Guardrail:** fragments and unease only — never name the Hollow Court or deliver the apex truth here.

## Quest Type
City / Dungeon / Exploration / Mystery

## Level Range
6–10. Solo danger: moderate, telegraphed, retreat-rich (see `THE_SUNKEN_WARDS.md` for full dungeon mechanics).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Find/open the Sealgate route: Streetwise DC 13 (or hire Old Pater Dunk).
- Navigate the Wards: Survival DC 14 (or Dunk/the Remnant map removes it).
- Cross flooded halls: Athletics/Con DC 13; read the flood cycle DC 14.
- Spot/avoid collapsing stone: Perception DC 13 / Dex save DC 14.
- Read in-situ Concord Script: Investigation DC 13 + Concord Script fluency (the gate).
- Resolve a Remembrance apparition without combat: Religion/Persuasion DC 13 (a rite, a name, a blessing) — or withdraw.

## Starting Hook
The player learns there's an *older city* beneath Caradril — the Sunken Wards — and that it holds readable Concord Script, dormant relics, and (the Bellman or a turned scholar hints) a back-way into the Remnant's Sealed Archive. Old Pater Dunk at the Sealgate will guide the worthy down.

## Location
Caradril, the Sunken Wards (entered via the Sealgate in the Sill).

## Involved NPCs
Old Pater Dunk (guide), Lantern-girl Esha (his apprentice), occasionally Wessel Dree/Reclaimer agents near the Archive back-way, Syndicate runners in the dry upper Wards.

## Involved Factions
Concord Remnant (secret map, back-way, Reclaimer guards), the Hush/Salt Syndicate (upper-Wards routes), Hollow Court (faint network touch only — never named).

## Surface Problem
Explore the Sunken Wards for what they hold.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The Wards are a **minor, dormant cousin of the Reach's harvest-network nodes**, faintly warming as the master harvest wakes far off — proof the network is *continental* (C-M2 fragment, reinforcing M2/M5 without naming the Court). The in-situ Script gives M6 *fragments* (Act-paced — never the apex). The Remnant has secretly mapped the Wards and uses a back-way to the Sealed Archive (an alternative to `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE`'s front door). A dormant relic offers an M3 demonstration. This is a *smaller echo* of the Reach's Deep Adit — never the keystone.

## Objectives
1. Get down into the Wards (Dunk, the Sealgate, or the Remnant route).
2. Read the Script / find a relic / find the Archive back-way (player's choice of goal).
3. Get out (retreat always available up the Sealgate).

## Possible Approaches
- **Explore & read:** Script fluency + Dunk's guidance for the M2/M6 fragments.
- **Loot:** recover a dormant relic (M3; thin-touch care).
- **Back-way:** find and use the route into the Sealed Archive (feeds `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE`).
- **Map it:** for the Remnant (with strings) or against them (exposing their secret).

## Obstacles
The Wards' hazards (flood, collapse, thin-touch, getting lost); avoidable Remembrance apparitions; Reclaimer guards near the Archive back-way; Syndicate runners in the upper Wards.

## Important Scenes
- The descent past the Sealgate into the still, listening dark.
- Concord Script on a drowned lintel, now readable — the network revealed as continental (M2 fragment).
- An apparition drifting up a flooded hall, telegraphed and avoidable.
- The hidden door to the Sealed Archive's lower connection — the Remnant's secret laid bare.

## Combat Options
None required. 1–2 Remembrance apparitions (specter/ghost-like, avoidable/parley-able); Reclaimer guards (turn-back, not kill); Syndicate runners (avoidable/bribable). Built for avoidance and cleverness, not a damage-race.

## Noncombat Options
Stealth, navigation, ritual/parley with apparitions, and social handling of guards/runners. The Wards can be explored without a fight.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
A guide (Dunk) removes the navigation risk; all hazards telegraphed; apparitions avoidable/parley-able; retreat up the Sealgate always available; the Wards are a *bonus* path, never a required gate (M2/M3/M6 reachable elsewhere).

## Rest And Time Pressure
Risky rest in dry upper chambers; safe rest by retreating to the Sill. Mild time pressure: the Remnant notices intruders and tightens the back-way; the Wards warm slowly over the campaign.

## Rewards
- **C-M2 fragment** (the network is continental — reinforces M2/M5).
- **C-M6 fragment** (in-situ Concord inscriptions; Act-paced).
- A dormant Remembrance relic (M3; thin-touch care) and Concord salvage/coin.
- The Sealed Archive back-way (worth more than gold — feeds `Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE`).

## Consequences
- **Read/map it:** the player confirms the network is continental and may gain the Archive back-way; the Remnant tightens security (Reclamation reacts).
- **Loot it:** M3 in hand; the Ledger/Remnant may want the relic.
- **Expose the Wards:** the city must face the ruin beneath it (a civic shock; reformers gain).
- **Ignore it:** the Wards keep warming unobserved; nothing lost permanently.

## Failure State
A failed delve costs time, gear, and maybe a thin-touch (reversible) — not a life, if the player heeds the telegraphs and retreats. The Wards' clues remain reachable elsewhere; no dead-end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
`Q_THE_SEALED_ARCHIVE` (via the back-way), M7 convergence (the continental network + M6 + M4), the Remnant's reaction, the civic fallout of exposing the Wards.

## Scaling Notes
- L6: fewer/weaker apparitions, simpler hazards, no Reclaimer ambush.
- L10: more apparitions, a Reclaimer attempt near the Archive, a flooded deeper hall with a stronger apparition guarding a better relic; never a damage-race, always a non-combat path.
