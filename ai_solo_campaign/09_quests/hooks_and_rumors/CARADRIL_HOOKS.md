## Notes

- **Entry hooks (CH1, CH2, CH15, CH10):** good first reasons to come to and move through Caradril.
- **Act pacing:** CH17/CH19 (the Archive, Quorrin) and the Sunken Wards (CH36) are Act-2/3 weight; give fragments, never the apex Hollow Court reveal, in early visits.
- Every hook points to a city quest, district, NPC, or authored clue — none are dead-ends.
