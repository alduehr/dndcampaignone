Surface through fiction (the clan-camps, the trade-meet, the open grass, the shrine-circle), never as a menu. Cast from `08_npcs/by_region/WENDER_STEPPE_NPCS.md`. The steppe is the **"outside view"** — a people outside the Concord's whole history. NO Concord ruins here; the Sky-Stones are independent. The "thinning sky" is an EXTREMELY oblique cosmic echo. No NPC knows the apex truth. 10 hooks + 8 rumors + 5 jobs.
