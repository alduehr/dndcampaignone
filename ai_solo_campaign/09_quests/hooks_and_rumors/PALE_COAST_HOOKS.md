## AI Use

Region-wide hooks for the Pale Coast (the Reach's western, sea-facing neighbor). Surface through fiction (jobs, NPCs in need, postings, harbor-talk), never as a menu. Each lists level, source, surface goal, authored content, and hidden link (DM-only). **The maritime node and the Lamp/Skerries truth are gated/oblique — never name the Hollow Court or the harvest mechanism; cap reveals.** 22 hooks.
