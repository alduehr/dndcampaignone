## AI Use

The rumor table "lives" in fiction at the **Pale Star** (Magisterium), the **Last Tally** (Quays), and the **Brass Ferry** (Sill), plus each district's hub. Truth Status guides how the AI DM handles them — some are true leads, some are propaganda, some are misdirection that still points at real content. Never reveal the DM "Points Toward" to the player. 30 rumors (CR1–CR30).
