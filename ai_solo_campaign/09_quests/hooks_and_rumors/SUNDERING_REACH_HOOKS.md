## Hook Distribution Notes

- **Low-level on-ramps (1–2):** H11, H16, H17, H23, H37 — gentle, near Hollowmere or the central road.
- **Investigation/social (2–4):** H12, H14, H19, H21, H24, H28, H32, H38 — the political/mystery core of Act 1–2.
- **Higher danger (3–5):** H30, H34, H35, H36 — the Greyfens deep and the Sunder Heights; telegraph and gate.
- Every hook points to an **authored** settlement, wilderness file, dungeon, or developed quest — no destination-less hooks.
