## AI Use
Develops Hook 2 ("Salt for the Sill"). A low-level travel/social quest west to Saltmargin to secure rite-salt — and the player's first taste of the Ledger's salt-profiteering and the salvage-skim thread.

## Quest Type
Regional / Travel

## Level Range
1–2. Solo danger: low (one telegraphed road encounter possible).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Negotiate a fair salt price: Persuasion DC 15 (DC 12 with Decca Rull's backing).
- Spot Marlow's rigged scale: Insight/Investigation DC 14.
- Survive a Pale Road bandit/smuggler encounter: social DC 13 or a brief CR-appropriate fight.
- Win Gull's trust for a shortcut: Persuasion DC 15.

## Starting Hook
Grandmother Wend (or a frightened Hollowmere family) sends the player west: the rite-salt is running out as failures spike, and the Coast traders are gouging. Fetch a load from Saltmargin — or recover a shipment that never arrived.

## Location
Hollowmere → Pale Road → Saltmargin (~2 days). The Broken Span and the Weigh-House are the key sites.

## Involved NPCs
Grandmother Wend (giver), Iss Marlow (Ledger; rigged scale), Decca Rull (ally), Gull Heddwyn (shortcut/contraband), Sister-of-salt Onna.

## Involved Factions
Mourners' Circle (need the salt), Cinder Ledger (controls and gouges it).

## Surface Problem
Get rite-salt to Hollowmere before more rites fail.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
Salt only *slows* failing rites (M5 context); the shortage panic is partly Ledger-manufactured value, and Marlow is skimming Concord salvage (M3 thread). The "missing shipment" variant may have been quietly diverted by Gull's smugglers.

## Objectives
1. Reach Saltmargin.
2. Secure a salt load (buy, recover, or liberate).
3. Get it back to Hollowmere intact.

## Possible Approaches
- **Buy it** (haggle Marlow down; or pay his gouge and resent it).
- **Earn it** (help Decca's crew or the Brine Chapel for a discount/donation).
- **Recover a stolen shipment** (track Gull's smugglers; negotiate or take it back).
- **Expose Marlow's skim** to leverage a fair price (ties to "Light on the Scale").

## Obstacles
The gouged price; a Pale Road bandit toll or smuggler ambush (telegraphed); the Broken Span detour; Marlow's rigged scale.

## Important Scenes
- The Weigh-House: Marlow's beautiful, crooked smile and beautiful, crooked scale.
- A near-riot at the salt-rate; Decca's crew on the edge of a strike.
- A night offer on the Tar Wharf from Gull.

## Clues
- The salt "only slows" the dead (Onna, Decca) — a quiet M5 seed.
- Marlow's skim — an M3/Monopoly-clock thread (C-M3-3-adjacent).

## Combat Options
A Pale Road encounter: **2–3 bandits** (CR 1/8, AC 12, ~11 HP) demanding a toll, or smugglers defending a diverted shipment. Telegraphed; avoidable by road-craft or coin. Solo-safe.

## Noncombat Options
Haggling, earning goodwill, exposing the scale, smuggler negotiation, or simply paying. No fight required.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
The road is scoutable; Crook the carter or Gull can guide; bandits prefer toll to murder; the player can always pay and leave.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rests at the Ashwalk Rest (en route) and the Knotted Hand. Soft time pressure: Hollowmere's rites worsen the longer the salt is delayed (advances the Mourners' clock, not a hard fail).

## Rewards
- ~40–60 gp value of rite-salt (or its delivery fee), plus Mourners' goodwill (toward Wend's trust / Secret 15).
- If Marlow is exposed: Decca's lasting alliance and a discount line for the Reach.
- Gull contact unlocked (black-market access).

## Consequences
- **Success:** Hollowmere's rites stabilize briefly; Mourners warm to the player.
- **Partial:** salt arrives short or late; rites fail worse; Wend still appreciative.
- **Ignored:** the salt crisis deepens; the Mourners' clock advances; Saltmargin's strike risk rises.
- **Excessive force:** robbing Marlow or the smugglers makes the Ledger an enemy.

## Failure State
If the player fails to bring any salt, a Hollowmere rite fails badly (a new local crisis hook) and the Mourners' Circle clock ticks — but the quest reopens via Saltmargin or another source. No dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
"Light on the Scale" (Marlow's skim); Decca's strike; Gull's "Grave-Goods"; deeper Mourners' trust (M6 path).

## Scaling Notes
- L1: skip the bandit fight; pure social/travel.
- L3–4: smugglers become a real fight; add a Ledger enforcer protecting Marlow's skim; raise relic stakes in the diverted shipment.
