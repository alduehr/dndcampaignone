## AI Use
Develops AV-H17. The Vale's toughest open job: lay the wrathful gibbet-dead at Marrow Cross and confront the Gravecaller recruiter exploiting the despair there. The Vale's combat/ritual peak short of the tomb, and where the region's cult-seed is found.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (wrathful dead; Vale cult-seed)

## Level Range
3–5. Solo danger: moderate (wrathful Remembrances). Telegraphed.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Lay a wrathful gibbet-dead by giving it its denied rites: Religion DC 15 (DC 13 with a Mourner).
- Resist the gibbet-cold/fear: Wis DC 13.
- Identify and confront the Gravecaller recruiter: Insight DC 13 / Persuasion DC 14.
- Learn the recruiter's truths without being recruited: Insight DC 13.

## Starting Hook
Gibbet-keeper Sorle (or the Mourners) reports the hanged dead "walking" and a stranger meeting the desperate at the crossroads after dark. Travelers won't pass Marrow Cross anymore; someone must settle it.

## Location
Marrow Cross (the gibbet crossroads; `ASHGARDEN_VALE_SITES.md`).

## Involved NPCs
Gibbet-keeper Sorle (minor; grim guide), the Gravecaller recruiter (the Vale cult-seed; a Sister-Knell-style figure), the wrathful dead.

## Surface Problem
Settle the walking gibbet-dead and make the crossroads safe.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The gibbet-dead are wrathful because they were *denied proper rites* — and the harvest's leakage feeds their anger (M5). The Gravecaller recruiter offers the despairing "a last word" and a fragment of the truth (M3 door), the leading edge of the Vale's first cell. **R1/R2-paced: the recruiter knows fragments, not the apex; never name the Court.**

## Objectives
1. Lay the wrathful dead (by giving them rites, not just fighting). 2. Find the recruiter. 3. Decide: stop them, learn from them, or help the despairing some other way.

## Possible Approaches
- **Ritual:** give the denied dead true rites (the right way; defuses the anger).
- **Combat:** fight the wrathful dead (possible, costly, and only a stopgap — they return unless ritualed).
- **Social:** confront or learn from the recruiter; protect the despairing (e.g., Widow Hessit).

## Obstacles
The wrathful dead; the gibbet-cold; the recruiter's persuasive grief-truths; the moral pull of "a last word."

## Important Scenes
- The gibbet at moonrise: chains, crows, cold, and the hanged walking.
- A true rite for a criminal nobody mourned — the quest's moral core.
- The recruiter, gentle and convincing, offering the player (or a grieving local) forbidden comfort.

## Clues
- The denied-rites cause of the wrath (M5; the harvest feeds anger).
- The recruiter's fragment of the truth (M3 door; the Gravecaller thread).

## Combat Options
Wrathful gibbet-dead (Wraith-like, AC 13, ~36 HP; 1–2, rarely more at night) — telegraphed; far better *settled by rites* than fought. Retreat to daylight/Marrow Cross ends danger. The recruiter avoids combat.

## Noncombat Options
Ritual, social, and protective approaches resolve the quest more fully than combat.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Daylight is safe; Sorle warns of the worst nights; a Mourner can aid the rite; retreat is always open; the recruiter is a talker, not a fighter.

## Rest And Time Pressure
No safe rest at Marrow Cross (haunting); Orchardmere/Tilbrook are a few hours off. Soft pressure: each week the recruiter wins another despairing soul (Vale cult-seed grows).

## Rewards
- The crossroads made safe; travelers and the Mourners grateful; ~40–60 gp.
- The Gravecaller fragment (M3 door) if the player engages the recruiter.
- A saved despairing local (e.g., Hessit) if protected in time.

## Consequences
- **Ritual + recruiter stopped/redeemed:** the cross rests; the cult-seed dies here; the Mourners honor the player.
- **Combat only:** the dead return; the recruiter keeps working; a stopgap.
- **Ignored:** Marrow Cross becomes impassable; the recruiter founds the Vale's first Gravecaller cell; the despairing radicalize.

## Failure State
If the player can't lay all the dead, the safe-passage window narrows but the cross stays passable by day; the thread reopens with Mourner aid. If the recruiter escapes, the cult-seed survives elsewhere (a future hook), not a dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
"A Last Word in the Vale" (AV-H13); the Vale Gravecaller cell (links to the Reach Gravecallers / M3 / M6 truth network, gated); the Mourners' deeper threads.

## Scaling Notes
- L3: 1 wrathful dead; emphasize ritual.
- L4–5: 2 wrathful dead on dark nights; the recruiter more established; raise the rite DCs slightly.
