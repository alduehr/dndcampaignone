## AI Use
Develops TW-H6. The mid-wood's dark heart: confront or learn from the Gravecaller cell and the old forest dead at the Hanging Oaks. Delivers the Tollwood's clearest oblique M5/M6 fragment short of the gated Old Mast, and decides whether the cell is stopped, befriended, or pushed deeper.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M5/M6 oblique) / Social

## Level Range
4–6. Solo danger: moderate (old hard-to-lay dead; the cell if made hostile). Telegraphed.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Enter the grove respectfully (avoid the dread/taboo): Wis DC 13 + an offering.
- Parley with the cell: Insight/Persuasion DC 13 (they want a witness).
- Honor/parley the oldest answering dead: Insight/Persuasion DC 15.
- Lay (not honor) a forest Remembrance: Religion DC 15 (some resist entirely).

## Starting Hook
The Gravecaller cell sheltered in the wood (or Sennet's fearful warning, or the search for Wren-of-the-Wood) draws the player to the Hanging Oaks — where the deep dead answer and the cult listens.

## Location
The Hanging Oaks (`THE_HANGING_OAKS.md`), in the mid-wood beyond Coldhearth.

## Involved NPCs
The Gravecaller cell speaker, Wren-of-the-Wood (drawn in), the oldest answering dead, Goodwife Sennet (warning from afar).

## Involved Factions
Gravecallers (the cell; the deep dead), Mourners'/woodfolk (the bargain), Ashen Wardens (rare; the old dead resist them).

## Surface Problem
What is the cult doing in the grove, and what do the old dead say?

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The grove is pre-Concord; its oldest dead testify that **"the grey lords took the dead before the roads fell"** — an oblique M5/M6 corroboration from before the Concord. The cell wants a witness and is edging toward waking the Old Mast (regional clock). **Gate it: a fragment and a dread, never the harvest's mechanism, never the Court.**

## Objectives
1. Reach and enter the grove respectfully. 2. Engage the cell and the old dead. 3. Decide: learn, stop, redirect, or exploit — and whether the cell goes deeper.

## Possible Approaches
- **Reverent/parley:** honor the grove and the oldest dead; earn the M5/M6 fragment; the best path.
- **Social (cell):** engage the Gravecallers as a witness (gain lore; risk being drawn into their recklessness).
- **Protective:** retrieve Wren-of-the-Wood; warn the village; check the cult.
- **Confront:** oppose the cell (only if hostile — turns the whole grove dangerous).

## Obstacles
The grove-dread; the taboo against defiling the offerings; old dead that resist laying; the cell's pull toward the deep; the moral weight of "the answering dead."

## Important Scenes
- The offering-edge: dread, old cords, the choice to enter with respect.
- The cell speaking with the deep dead — and inviting the player to listen.
- The oldest dead's alien testimony, and its price.

## Clues
- The grove's pre-Concord nature (Nature/Religion; the deep secret's shape).
- The cell's teaching (oblique M6 fragment).
- The oldest dead's testimony (the richest oblique M5/M6 fragment short of the Old Mast; gated).

## Combat Options
Old forest dead (Specter-like, older, AC 13, ~30 HP; hard to lay) and, if provoked, the cell (Cult Fanatic-like + recruits) and the whole roused grove. **All avoidable** — reverence and parley are the design. See the dungeon.

## Noncombat Options
Reverence, offering, and parley resolve the quest more fully than any fight.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Open retreat to the offering-edge; the old dead don't pursue past it; the cell lets the respectful leave; an offering atones for offense; Sennet's forest-rules ease the dread.

## Rest And Time Pressure
No safe rest in the grove unless the cell vouches and an offering is made; Coldhearth is the recovery point. Soft pressure: the cell edges toward the Old Mast (regional clock).

## Rewards
- The oblique M5/M6 fragment (a major, gated clue).
- The cell as a wary contact (forbidden lore) or its recklessness checked; Wren-of-the-Wood saved.
- The Hung Tree's favor (a one-time boon, earned by reverence).

## Consequences
- **Reverent + cell checked:** the grove quiets; the cult's push toward the Old Mast slows; the woodfolk honor the player.
- **Exploited (cell's way):** more lore, but the cult goes deeper — advancing the Old Mast clock.
- **Confronted/defiled:** the grove turns hostile; the woodfolk turn cold; the fragment is lost.
- **Ignored:** the cell pushes deeper unopposed; the old dead spread; the Old Mast clock advances.

## Failure State
If the player can't earn the fragment, they keep the grove's *shape* (older than the Concord; the dead remember "the taking") and the thread reopens at the Old Mast. If they rouse the grove and flee, they escape marked, not dead. No dead end.

## Scaling Notes
- L4: lean on dread, parley, the cell; soften the old dead.
- L6: the grove more awake; the cell more committed; the fragment comes with a clear Old-Mast warning.
