## AI Use
The Candlewick archive-stone quest. The Concord Remnant wants to buy and remove the village's Concord record-wall; the player decides its fate — and may begin learning Concord Script (the key clue gate). Pairs with Hook 8 / the Whispering Cairn.

## Quest Type
Regional / Social / Investigation

## Level Range
2–3. Solo danger: very low (a safe village; a possible rival snatch at the cairn).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Read the archive-stone (gated): Investigation DC 13 with Concord Script/Comprehend Languages, or with Briss reading.
- Persuade Hayward Greel to keep/sell the stone: Persuasion DC 14.
- Expose the Remnant's true interest to Briss: Insight DC 13 + evidence.
- Begin learning Concord Script: a downtime activity with Briss (no single roll).

## Starting Hook
Lector Briss is in Candlewick studying the archive-stone — and the Remnant has quietly offered Hayward Greel coin to "purchase and protect" it. The Mourners and some villagers are uneasy. Should the Reach's most readable Concord text leave?

## Location
Candlewick: the Green/Archive-Stone; the Whispering Cairn (related reader-site on the South Road).

## Involved NPCs
Lector Briss (honest scholar/dupe), Hayward Greel (tempted), Mourner Edda (opposes the sale), a Remnant agent (Veska Dunn or a courier), Chapel-clerk Vesna (curious).

## Involved Factions
Concord Remnant (wants the stone), Compact (Greel decides), Mourners' Circle (the dead's records belong to the dead).

## Surface Problem
Decide and influence whether Candlewick sells its archive-stone.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
A panel of the stone is a fragment of the harvest-truth (M6/M9 seed). The Remnant wants it because Quorrin's inner circle knows what it says. Briss does not — he thinks he's preserving heritage. The village's rites "hold" because Candlewick sits at the *edge* of the shrine's pull (M5 geography), readable in the stone.

## Objectives
1. Engage with the stone (read it, or learn Script via Briss).
2. Influence the sale (keep, sell, or copy-then-keep).
3. Optionally, plant doubt in Briss about his own faction.

## Possible Approaches
- **Scholarly:** read/copy the stone; learn Concord Script (the gate to most deep clues).
- **Social:** sway Greel; rally the Mourners and villagers.
- **Investigation:** uncover the Remnant's real motive and show Briss.
- **Compromise:** make a rubbing for the Reach and let (or refuse) the sale.

## Obstacles
Greel's temptation; the language gate; the Remnant's coin and patience; village factionalism (pride vs. dread).

## Important Scenes
- Briss on the green, lighting up then troubled as he half-reads a line.
- Greel, over mead, testing the player's view of "the Caradril offer."
- The moment Briss first suspects his own order wants this for the wrong reason.

## Clues
- **C-M6/M9 fragment** (the stone's panel; language-gated).
- **C-M5 geography** (why Candlewick "holds").
- Briss as a future ally lever (Secret 13).

## Combat Options
None in the village. If the player escorts Briss to the Whispering Cairn, a Remnant snatch-team or scavengers may contest it (see that dungeon) — telegraphed, avoidable.

## Noncombat Options
Entirely social/scholarly. The "fight" is over influence and knowledge.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Candlewick is a safe-village hub with real healing (the Threshold chapel); no forced combat; the Script gate is reachable here or via Briss elsewhere.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest and divine healing at Candlewick. Time pressure: if the player dawdles, Greel may sell (removing the stone and strengthening the Reclamation clock).

## Rewards
- **Concord Script tutelage** from Briss (the single most useful clue-key in the campaign — a downtime reward).
- A stone-rubbing (portable M6/M9 fragment).
- Greel's, the Mourners', or the Remnant's goodwill (by choice).
- Briss as a potential future ally (Secret 13 groundwork).

## Consequences
- **Keep it:** the Reach retains its most accessible Concord text; the Remnant resents the player; Briss stays.
- **Sell it:** Quorrin gains the panel; the Reclamation clock advances; the Mourners are betrayed.
- **Copy-then-keep:** best of both, at the cost of Remnant suspicion.
- **Disillusion Briss:** seeds the Remnant's future fracture (Secret 13).

## Failure State
If the stone is sold and uncopied, the M6/M9 fragment is still reachable (Caradril archives, the Deep Adit, the Barrow), but a friendly, low-risk path is lost and the Remnant strengthens. No dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
Learning more Concord Script (every gated clue opens up); the Whispering Cairn; the Remnant's Caradril archives (Act 3); Briss's disillusionment arc.

## Scaling Notes
- L2: pure social/scholarly; no rivals.
- L4: the Remnant sends Veska Dunn to take the stone by maneuver; a cairn confrontation; higher persuasion DCs.
