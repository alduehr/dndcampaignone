## AI Use
Develops AV-H3. The Vale's clearest **M3** path: the "saint's relics" sold to pilgrims and bought by the Ledger are Remembrance relics — they store the dead. Investigation-led, with a moral reckoning over relic-fraud and grave-robbing.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M3)

## Level Range
2–4. Solo danger: low-moderate (the Pellow Grange infiltration).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Confirm a "saint's relic" stores a dead person: Arcana/Religion DC 13 (or experiential — hold it).
- Trace the relics to their source: Investigation DC 13 (Relic Row → Pell → Pellow Grange).
- Get Pell to admit the trade: Insight/Persuasion DC 18 (DC 12 with the dig-ledger as evidence).
- Infiltrate the Pellow Grange: Stealth DC 13 / Persuasion DC 14 (see the dungeon).

## Starting Hook
Mother Combe (or a troubled pilgrim) asks the player to find out whether the "saint's bones" sold at Saint Veddow's are holy relics or something blasphemous. She suspects the latter and dreads of it in her own bones.

## Location
Orchardmere (Pell's counting-yard) → Saint Veddow's (Relic Row) → the Pellow Grange.

## Involved NPCs
Mother Ezrith Combe (giver), Factor Wymar Pell (Ledger; culprit), Relic-monger Coyle Tamm, Foreman Drust (grange).

## Involved Factions
Mourners' Circle (horror at the blasphemy), Cinder Ledger (the relic-trade and Monopoly clock).

## Surface Problem
Are the saint's relics real or fake — and if fake, what are they?

## Hidden Truth (DM)
They are **Remembrance relics** quarried from Concord field-shrines (M3), sold as saint's-bones to pilgrims and, more lucratively, bought up by the Ledger for shipment south to corner the market (Monopoly clock; ties to Caradril). Pell knows exactly what they are. **R2/M3 is Act-2 paced — at low levels deliver the *experiential* truth (the relic shows a dead face) without the full "someone is collecting the dead" frame.**

## Objectives
1. Test a relic and confirm it holds a dead person.
2. Trace the supply to Pell and the Pellow Grange.
3. Decide: expose the fraud, stop the quarrying, or profit from it.

## Possible Approaches
- **Scholarly/experiential:** test a relic (the dead face); convince Combe and the Mourners.
- **Investigative:** follow the supply chain to the grange dig-ledger (the documentary proof).
- **Confrontational/social:** force Pell to admit it (hard without evidence).

## Obstacles
Pell's charm and deniability; the pilgrim-trade's vested interest; the grange's guarded dig.

## Important Scenes
- A relic in the player's hand showing a stranger's dying face (M3, experiential).
- Pell, warm and untroubled, offering the player good coin to bring him "any old bones."
- Combe's grief and fury when she learns her faith's relics are the stolen dead.

## Clues
- The relic stores a person (C-AV-M3-1).
- Pell's dig-ledger logs relics by grave (the Pellow Grange; M3 documentary).
- Shipping-labels south (Monopoly clock; Caradril thread).

## Combat Options
Avoidable. The grange's dig-crew (Thug-like, AC 11, ~32 HP) prefer threats; combat is the failure path. See `THE_PELLOW_GRANGE.md`.

## Noncombat Options
The whole quest is investigation, testing, and social maneuver; no fight required.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
The relic-test is rollable or experiential; the grange is scoutable; the player can gather proof and leave; Combe and Tilbrook back a careful player.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rests in Orchardmere, Saint Veddow's, Tilbrook. Soft pressure: each week Pell ships another cart south (Monopoly clock).

## Rewards
- M3 understanding (a major campaign clue, act-capped).
- Combe's and the Mourners' deep trust; Tilbrook's gratitude if the quarrying is stopped.
- Coin if the player instead profits (at a moral cost the Mourners remember).

## Consequences
- **Expose/stop:** the Vale relic-trade falters; the Ledger's Monopoly clock eases; Pell becomes an enemy.
- **Profit:** the player gains coin and a Ledger favor; the Mourners turn cold.
- **Ignored:** Pell ships more relics south, advancing the Monopoly clock toward Caradril.

## Failure State
If Pell evades exposure, the trade continues — but the player keeps the experiential M3 truth, and the thread reopens at the Pellow Grange or via Tilbrook. No dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
"The Marks on the Doors" (Pellow Grange); "The Ledger's Quiet Cart" (AV-H19); the Monopoly clock toward Caradril; Combe's deeper Mourner threads (M6, gated).

## Scaling Notes
- L2: experiential M3 only; the grange lightly guarded.
- L4: full supply-chain; a Ledger enforcer at the grange; relics worth fighting (and not) over.
