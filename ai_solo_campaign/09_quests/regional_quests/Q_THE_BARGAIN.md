## AI Use
Develops TW-H10. The trust-and-lore quest that unlocks the deep Tollwood: earn Coldhearth's confidence, learn the forest-rules, and (gated) hear the wood's deep history from Goodwife Sennet. The gateway to the Hanging Oaks and the Old Mast, and the Tollwood's oblique M5/M6 fragment-source.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M5/M6 oblique; gated) / Social

## Level Range
3–5. Solo danger: low-moderate (the journey to Coldhearth; the mid-wood).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Earn Coldhearth's hospitality/trust: Persuasion DC 13 (lower with respect + a service done).
- Learn the forest-rules: Insight DC 10 (freely taught to the respectful).
- Earn Sennet's old songs (gated): Persuasion/Insight DC 16 (DC 13 with deep earned trust).
- Survive the mid-wood to reach Coldhearth: Survival DC 14 (much lower with Brann).

## Starting Hook
To go deeper into the wood — and understand why it's turning dangerous — the player must win over Coldhearth, the village that keeps the old forest-bargain. Brann, the Mourners, or the Marked Oaks quest points them here; Goodwife Sennet decides whether they're worthy.

## Location
Coldhearth and the mid-wood approach.

## Involved NPCs
Goodwife Sennet (forest-elder; the deep secret's keeper), Brann the Guide, Old Gethin, Wren-of-the-Wood, the sheltered Gravecaller cell.

## Involved Factions
Mourners'/woodfolk (the bargain), Gravecallers (sheltered; the deep dead).

## Surface Problem
Earn the woodfolk's trust and the knowledge to go deeper safely.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
Coldhearth keeps the old bargain that holds the **Old Mast's presence** asleep; Sennet's songs hold a **pre-Concord witness to "the taking"** — the Tollwood's oblique M5/M6 fragment. **Gate it: deliver the forest-rules and a *fragment/dread* of the deep history, not the harvest's mechanism, not the Court. The full Old Mast truth stays at the Old Mast.**

## Objectives
1. Reach Coldhearth and earn hospitality. 2. Learn the forest-rules (the safety key). 3. (Gated) earn Sennet's songs / the deep history.

## Possible Approaches
- **Service:** do honest work for the village (help the burns, find Wren, defend a Coppice Shrine) to earn trust.
- **Respect:** keep the customs flawlessly; let Sennet test you.
- **Curiosity:** engage the sheltered Gravecallers (a faster, riskier route to the deep lore — Sennet disapproves).

## Obstacles
The village's wariness of outsiders; the customs (easily broken by the careless); the journey; Sennet's high bar for the old songs.

## Important Scenes
- Earning a place at the Guides' Fire — or being turned into the dark for scoffing.
- Sennet, by firelight, weighing whether the player is worthy of the songs.
- The Gravecaller cell's quiet, dangerous invitation to go deeper against Sennet's warning.

## Clues
- The forest-rules (the wood's safety logic; survival).
- Sennet's songs (gated oblique M5/M6 fragment — "the grey lords took the dead").
- The bargain and the Old Mast (the deep secret's shape; gated).

## Combat Options
None required. The journey may bring mid-wood predators or old dead (telegraphed; see the Tollwood table); within Coldhearth, no combat by default.

## Noncombat Options
The entire quest is social, service, and survival.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Brann guides; the village wants worthy outsiders to learn; the customs are taught freely; the gated lore is optional and the player is never blocked from the rest of the wood by failing it.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe hearth-rest for an honored guest; the mid-wood is unsafe. Soft pressure: the deep-cutting and the Gravecallers' recklessness advance the Old Mast clock while the player earns trust.

## Rewards
- The forest-rules (real safety in the deep wood thereafter — eases Survival/dread DCs).
- Brann/Coldhearth as allies; folk healing and herb-lore.
- (Gated) Sennet's oblique M5/M6 fragment; the gateway to the Hanging Oaks and Old Mast threads.

## Consequences
- **Trust earned:** the deep wood opens to the player safely; Coldhearth backs them.
- **Customs broken:** the player is shut out, the wood grows hostile to them, and the deep lore passes to the reckless Gravecallers.
- **Ignored:** Coldhearth's bargain frays unaided; the cult and the Ledger push the Old Mast toward waking.

## Failure State
If the player can't earn the songs, they still get the forest-rules and safe-ish passage; the deep history reopens via the Hanging Oaks or the Old Mast. No dead end.

## Scaling Notes
- L3: trust-and-rules focus; the songs lightly gated.
- L5: the deep history available; the Old Mast clock pressing; the Gravecaller temptation sharper.
