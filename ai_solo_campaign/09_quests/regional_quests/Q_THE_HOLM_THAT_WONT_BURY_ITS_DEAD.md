## AI Use
The Greywater Holm crisis quest. A family's rite keeps failing; helping them is the clearest Act 1 field-proof of M5 (rites fail worst near the water) and a gentle door to both the Wardens (Secret 8 path) and the Gravecallers (Knell).

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery / Social

## Level Range
2–3. Solo danger: low-to-moderate (a single telegraphed failed-rite Remembrance).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Comfort/learn from Widow Sarn: Insight/Persuasion DC 12.
- Diagnose why the rite fails: Religion/Medicine DC 13 (Warden aid lowers it).
- Complete a true rite: Religion DC 14 (auto with a Warden's help).
- Earn Elder Mabb's trust: Persuasion DC 13.
- Notice the grave-drift (M5): Investigation/Survival DC 12.

## Starting Hook
In Greywater Holm, Widow Sarn's husband's rite has failed three times — the dead won't go down. The village is breaking. Help, before they turn to the only ones who promise an answer.

## Location
Greywater Holm: the Crooked Longhall, the Creeping Ground (burial-rise), Sashe's hut nearby.

## Involved NPCs
Widow Sarn (the grieving), Elder Mabb Tay (heretical elder), Sister Knell (the gentle cult-door), Old Sashe (the drift-witness), a Warden (Hale/Pell) if fetched.

## Involved Factions
Mourners' Circle (failing), Ashen Wardens (overwhelmed), Gravecallers (offering "a kinder way").

## Surface Problem
Lay one man to rest and steady a frightened village.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The rite fails because the shrine's pull is strongest here (M5); Sarn's husband's grave drifts waterward like Wren's. The Gravecallers can "quiet" the dead (not lay them) — comfort that hides a deeper danger. This is the campaign's clearest early demonstration that the failures are *geographic* and worsening toward the basin.

## Objectives
1. Help with the failing rite (or learn why it fails).
2. Steady the village (or watch it turn to the cult).
3. Recognize the pattern (M5) — the dead are pulled toward the water.

## Possible Approaches
- **Ritual:** fetch or assist a Warden; complete a true rite (the "right" answer, but the pull resists).
- **Folk:** work with Elder Mabb and the old grief-songs.
- **Forbidden:** let Sister Knell show "the kinder way" (Gravecaller door; comfort with a hidden cost).
- **Investigation:** examine the drifting graves with Sashe (M5 proof).

## Obstacles
The rite's resistance (the pull); the village's despair; the cult's pull on the grieving; the elder's shame.

## Important Scenes
- A rite failing in the longhall — the dead sitting up, gentle and wrong.
- Sister Knell, grieving herself, offering Sarn (and the player) comfort the Wardens can't.
- Sashe at the Creeping Ground, showing how every grave leans toward the basin.

## Clues
- **C-M5-2 / C-M5-1** (grave-drift; Sashe's testimony) — the field-proof.
- A Warden noticing rites fail *worse here* (C-M5-4; Secret 8 seed).
- The Gravecallers' "quieting" (a forbidden M3/M6 hint via Knell).

## Combat Options
The failed-rite Remembrance (specter/ghost-like, AC 12, ~22 HP) — **non-combat primary** (true rite/parley). It is sorrowful, not aggressive; combat only if the village or player forces it.

## Noncombat Options
Ritual, folk-song, the cult's comfort, or simple presence and listening. No fight required; the "win" is mercy, not damage.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
A Warden can be fetched to handle the dead; Sashe guides; the Remembrance never ambushes; retreat to the longhall always available.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Spare safe rest in the longhall; full recovery at Hollowmere/Ashwalk Rest. Time pressure: each day un-helped, the village inches toward the cult (advances the Gravecaller clock) and Sarn's grief curdles.

## Rewards
- Mourners' and village goodwill (toward Wend's trust / Secret 15).
- A Warden-trust beat (toward Secret 8) if the player works with the order.
- The M5 clue — the single most important Act 1 realization.
- If the cult-door is taken: a gentle, low-risk entry to the Gravecallers (M3/M6).

## Consequences
- **Lay him truly:** the village's faith steadies; Mabb weeps with relief; a real, hard-won mercy.
- **Cult comfort:** the dead is "quieted" not freed; the village leans cult-ward; a hidden debt accrues.
- **Ignore:** Sarn joins the cell; the holm tips toward the Gravecallers; the drift thickens.

## Failure State
If the player can't resolve it, the Gravecallers step in (advancing their clock and Greywater's slide) — the M5 clue is still available via Sashe, and a new "save Greywater" hook opens. No dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
Sashe's drift-tour ("Sashe's Warning"); the Drowned Blind / Gravecaller cell ("The Second Mark"); the Barrow of Nine Doors (deep M5/M6); Warden-trust toward Othetha's confession.

## Scaling Notes
- L2: single weak Remembrance; generous DCs.
- L4: the pull is stronger (the rite needs two successes or a relic); add a Gravecaller standoff for Sarn's loyalty.
