## AI Use
The Kettle Bridge investigation quest. River-drownings at the broken arch lead to the Sunken Tollhouse — a clean M2/M5 clue site and an early demonstration that the crisis lives in the water.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery / Dungeon

## Level Range
2–3. Solo danger: low-to-moderate (water, cold, one telegraphed Remembrance).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Get Cray to authorize an off-books look: Persuasion DC 15.
- Pry the river-truth from Tibb: Insight/Persuasion DC 12.
- Navigate/dive the flooded ruin: Athletics DC 13; Survival DC 13.
- Read the Tollhouse Script (gated): Investigation DC 13 with Concord Script/Comprehend Languages.
- Lay or parley the drowned Remembrance: Religion DC 14 / Persuasion DC 13.

## Starting Hook
Boatmen are drowning at the bridge's broken arch — good swimmers, all of them. Bailiff Cray won't touch "ghost stories," but he'll quietly ask the player to look. Or Bargemaster Tibb begs help before he loses more crew.

## Location
Kettle Bridge → the broken fourth arch → the Sunken Tollhouse (see dungeon file).

## Involved NPCs
Bailiff Cray (deniable patron), Bargemaster Tibb (witness/guide), Toll-farmer Bly (looting the ruin), Bann Oester (the river-dead won't bury), Warden Pell (if fetched).

## Involved Factions
Compact (wants it quiet), Ledger (Bly's salvage), Gravecallers (a cutout among the bargemen).

## Surface Problem
Stop the drownings at the broken arch.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The Sunken Tollhouse is a leaking minor harvest node (M2/M5); its leakage drowns the boatmen and seeds Reedford's haunting downstream. Bly has been fishing salvage from it. A drowned boatman's Remembrance is jammed at the node.

## Objectives
1. Investigate the drownings (talk to witnesses, examine the arch).
2. Enter the Sunken Tollhouse.
3. Resolve the leak (re-settle the relay-stone; lay/parley the drowned dead).

## Possible Approaches
- **Investigation/social:** witnesses, the burial records, Bly's salvage.
- **Exploration:** dive/boat into the ruin (with Tibb's help).
- **Ritual:** fetch Warden Pell to re-settle the node, or do it yourself.
- **Forbidden:** follow the Gravecaller cutout for the dead's own account.

## Obstacles
Cold flooded ruin; rising-water clock; the drowned Remembrance; Bly's interference (she doesn't want the ruin scrutinized).

## Important Scenes
- A drowned man hauled from the arch as the player arrives.
- Tibb dropping his voice to describe what he's seen in the water.
- The Relay-Room: the grey light, the cold, the weeping dead boatman.

## Clues
- **C-M2 fragment** (Tollhouse Script): "the road carries them to the door under the water."
- **C-M5** (the relay-stone leak; the dead pulled downstream/basinward).
- Bly's salvage (M3 thread; links to Saltmargin/the river trade).

## Combat Options
The drowned Remembrance (specter-like, AC 12, ~22 HP) — **non-combat resolution primary** (parley/rite). A fen predator on the flooded stair (CR 1/8, optional). Telegraphed; escapable.

## Noncombat Options
Parley or lay the dead; re-settle the relay-stone; expose Bly; the whole quest can be solved without a kill.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Tibb guides; the stair stays open (retreat anytime); rising water is a clock, not a drowning-trap; Warden Pell can assist.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest at the Drowned Kettle inn; short rests on the dry stair. Time pressure: each season the drownings spread upriver (advances the basin/master clock regionally), and the rising-water clock pressures inside the ruin.

## Rewards
- ~25 gp old Concord coin; a minor Remembrance relic (M3); a drowned bargeman's signet (returns to a grieving family — goodwill, follow-up).
- Eased drownings (visible win); Cray's grudging favor; Tibb's loyalty.
- C-M2/M5 clues toward the central mystery.

## Consequences
- **Success:** drownings stop at the arch; the bridge-town steadies; Cray takes quiet credit.
- **Partial:** drownings ease but recur as the Court counters.
- **Ignored:** drownings spread to Reedford and upriver; the crossing is abandoned (closing the central road); Bly keeps looting.
- **Excessive force:** smashing the relay-stone *frees the dead violently* and worsens the leak (a Gravecaller-flavored mistake) — telegraphed as dangerous.

## Failure State
If the player can't resolve the node, the leak persists and a new river-haunting hook opens at Reedford — the mystery continues by another route. Bann Oester's "won't-keep" graves give an alternate M5 clue.

## Follow-Up Hooks
Reedford's grey woman (down-river link); Bly's salvage trade ("Toll and Trouble"); the Gravecaller river-cutout; Cray's political ambition ("The Bailiff's Ladder").

## Scaling Notes
- L1–2: single weak Remembrance; soft water DCs.
- L4–5: add a second drowned Remembrance and a thin-touch aura at the relay-stone; Bly's men actively obstruct.
