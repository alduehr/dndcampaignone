## AI Use
Develops AV-H9. The Vale's keystone quest: whether to open the inner tomb beneath Saint Veddow's. The clearest Ring 1 path to **what the Concord built** (M2/M6 fragments), gated to level 4–6 and Act 2–3 reveal pacing. The Warden seal-keeper (Adwen) and the Remnant scholar (Oneth Vael) pull the player two ways.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M2/M6 fragment)

## Level Range
4–6. Solo danger: moderate-to-high (the dungeon and the Tomb-Warden). Telegraphed.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Persuade Sister Adwen to allow / aid access: DC 16 (DC 12 if the player has shown the seal matters, via AV-H7).
- Gain Mother Sennet's sacred permission: DC 15.
- Find/use the Remnant's service-stair excavation: Investigation DC 14 / Stealth DC 13.
- Read the inner Script: Concord Script (reader required).
- Settle the Tomb-Warden by rite: Religion DC 15 (DC 13 with a Warden).

## Starting Hook
The Remnant's Sub-Lector Oneth Vael wants into the inner tomb and offers the player coin and scholarship to make it happen; or the player's own curiosity, chasing the failing rests (AV-H8), arrives at the sealed door. Either way, Warden Sister Adwen stands in the way — guarding on an instinct she cannot name.

## Location
Saint Veddow's Rest — the Shrine Crown, the Warden House, and the inner tomb (`SAINT_VEDDOWS_TOMB.md`).

## Involved NPCs
Sub-Lector Oneth Vael (Remnant; wants in), Warden Sister Adwen (seal-keeper; resists), Mother Sennet (shrine-keeper; torn), the Tomb-Warden (dungeon climax).

## Involved Factions
Concord Remnant (open it, study it), Ashen Wardens (keep it sealed), Mourners' Circle (let the dead rest).

## Surface Problem
Should the old door be opened — and who decides?

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The tomb is a **capped Concord shrine / secondary harvest node**; the Wardens unknowingly guard a seal (Adwen holds a fragment of the truth). Opening it yields M2/M6 *fragments* — proof the Concord built a continental network and "kept the dead." **Strictly gated: fragments and danger only; never the harvest's full purpose pre-Act-3, never the Hollow Court.** The Remnant wants the node for the Reclamation race.

## Objectives
1. Decide whose side you're on (open / keep shut / find your own way in).
2. Gain entry (persuade, permit, sneak, or — worst — force).
3. Survive the inner tomb and learn what you can.
4. Decide what to do with what you find and who gets the node.

## Possible Approaches
- **Diplomacy:** win Adwen's trust and aid (the safest, richest path — she becomes a guide and the rite-help against the Tomb-Warden).
- **Scholarship:** ally with Oneth Vael and the Remnant (opens the door, but advances their Reclamation interest).
- **Stealth:** use the Remnant's service-stair quietly (avoids the politics, risks sacrilege).
- **Force:** break the seal (sacrilege; rouses the Tomb-Warden hostile; thin-touch backlash). The failure-flavored path.

## Obstacles
Adwen's guarded refusal; Sennet's torn conscience; the cold, dread, and Tomb-Warden of the tomb; the moral weight of the Keeping-Cell relics.

## Important Scenes
- Adwen and Oneth Vael's quiet, courteous war over a door, and the player as the swing vote.
- The empty sarcophagus — *there is no saint here* (M2 shape).
- The Tomb-Warden's challenge in the Antechamber: fight, ritual, or parley.

## Clues
- The Keeping-Cell Script and relics (M2/M3 fragment).
- The Warden records (M6 fragment; conditional).
- The waking shaft (M2 fragment — the continental network; never the Court).

## Combat Options
The Tomb-Warden (Wraith/ghost-like, AC 13, ~52 HP at L5) — but ritual or parley resolves it better and yields richer clues. It cannot leave the node; retreat ends any fight. See the dungeon.

## Noncombat Options
Diplomacy, scholarship, stealth, and ritual carry the whole quest; the climax is best resolved without a blade.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Adwen as guide/healer; open retreat up the Cold Stair; the Warden House sanctuary; the sealed shaft explicitly off-limits and telegraphed lethal so a low-level player can't over-reach.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Safe rest only above, in the pilgrim hall / Warden House. No rest below. Soft pressure: if the player dithers, the Remnant gains access another way (advancing their Reclamation clock at this site).

## Rewards
- M2/M6 fragments (major, act-capped clues).
- Adwen's deep trust / a Warden seal-token (Warden rite-aid elsewhere) if befriended.
- Remnant favor / coin if allied with them (with strings).
- Keeping-Cell relics (M3; the recurring moral choice).

## Consequences
- **Opened with the Wardens:** the secret is shared, the seal respected; the node stays controlled; Adwen and the Wardens become allies.
- **Opened for the Remnant:** the Remnant begins working the node (Reclamation clock advances at a Vale site; a future problem).
- **Forced:** sacrilege; the Wardens, Mourners, and pilgrims turn on the player; the node leaks worse.
- **Kept shut:** the failing rests fester, but the node stays sealed and safe — a defensible choice.

## Failure State
If the player can't get in (or retreats), they keep the door's *telegraph* (M2 shape: there is no saint; something Concord and cold lies below) and the thread reopens. If they force it and flee the Tomb-Warden, they escape with a thin-touch scare and a fragment — bruised, not dead. No dead end.

## Scaling Notes
- L4: soften the Tomb-Warden; lean on the diplomacy/dread.
- L5 (intended): as written.
- L6+: the node hums louder; the Remnant is further in; the shaft stays gated regardless.
