## AI Use
Develops TW-H5. A low-level node-delve under Tollstone Cross: the leaking Concord road-node the Tollmen fear, source of the local dead-drift. Delivers M2/M5 fragments (the road is the machine) and a lever in the toll-war (clearing the Tollmen's dread earns their gratitude).

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M2/M5 fragment) / Dungeon

## Level Range
2–4. Solo danger: low-moderate (water, faint leakage, one layable dead).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Get past/below the Tollmen's avoided stair: Persuasion DC 12 (they're glad someone else goes) / Stealth DC 13.
- Read the conduit Script: Concord Script (reader required).
- Lay the drowned Remembrance: Religion DC 13.
- Navigate the flooded vault: Athletics DC 13.

## Starting Hook
The Tollmen won't go down "the cold stair" under the Broken Arch — but the dead drift near the road because of it, and Smith Doune (or Renn, wanting it gone) asks the player to find out what's down there.

## Location
The Greenward Toll-Station / Drowned Vault, below Tollstone Cross (`THE_GREENWARD_TOLL_STATION.md`).

## Involved NPCs
Smith Doune / Toll-Boss Cady Renn (givers), the drowned toll-keeper Remembrance.

## Surface Problem
Find and stop whatever draws the dead to this stretch.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The vault is a minor Concord road-node, leaking faintly as the keystone reawakens (M2/M5 fragment; the road is a conduit — mirrors the Reach's Sunken Tollhouse). **Cap at R1: a source and a pattern; the road carries the dead; never the harvest's full purpose, never the Court.**

## Objectives
1. Enter the drowned vault. 2. Find the leak (the node) and the drowned dead. 3. Settle/ease it; decide what to do with the clue and salvage.

## Possible Approaches
- **Exploration/ritual:** delve, read the Script, lay the drowned dead (eases the drift).
- **Salvage:** recover the strongbox and relic (for coin, the Ledger, or the Remnant).
- **Lever:** clear the Tollmen's dread to earn their gratitude (feeds the toll-war).

## Obstacles
The flooded footing; cold-damp; the faint leakage (thin-touch); a possible scavenger; the Tollmen's superstition.

## Important Scenes
- The cold stair the bandits won't descend.
- The flooded toll-hall and the drowned keeper drifting up toward the light.
- The conduit humming faintly — the road, alive.

## Clues
- The conduit Script (C-TW-M2-1; the road is the machine).
- The drowned dead's testimony ("something calls them west"; M5 fragment, oblique).
- The salvage relic (M3).

## Combat Options
A flooded-cellar scavenger (CR 1/4–1) and the drowned dead (Specter-like, AC 12, ~22 HP) — both avoidable/layable; combat is minor and telegraphed.

## Noncombat Options
Exploration, Script-reading, and ritual carry the quest; no fight required.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Open stair and a culvert exit; the dead are layable; the leakage is faint and reversible (thin-touch); retreat is easy.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Short rest in the dry upper stair; long rest at Tollstone Cross (uneasy) or Hartfell. No hard time pressure.

## Rewards
- M2/M5 fragments and an M3 relic.
- The toll-station strongbox (~70 gp) and road-salvage (Ledger/Remnant interest).
- The Tollmen's gratitude (a toll-war lever) for clearing their feared stair.

## Consequences
- **Cleared/laid:** the local drift eases; the Tollmen are grateful (toll-war lever); the Green Mile calms.
- **Salvaged only:** coin, but the node keeps leaking.
- **Ignored:** the drift worsens, the Green Mile grows eerier, and Skell exploits the Tollmen's fear.

## Failure State
If the player can't lay the drowned dead, the drift persists but the M2/M5 fragment stands and the thread reopens. No dead end.

## Scaling Notes
- L2: soft water-and-haunting delve.
- L4: a scavenger fight, a wrathful drowned dead, deeper thin-touch risk.
