## AI Use
Develops TW-H7/H15. The Tollwood's gated apex quest: a guided approach to the Old Mast edge — and possibly its Still Heart — to learn the deep wood's oblique truth and/or stop the presence being woken. **Higher-tier (level 6–8), heavily telegraphed, capped: the player earns dread, a fragment, and a warning — never the campaign's apex truth, never the Hollow Court.**

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M5/M6 oblique; gated) / Exploration

## Level Range
6–8. Solo danger: high and gated. The deep wood pushes the unworthy out alive; lethal only if the player escalates against a vastly superior power.

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Reach the Old Mast edge with a guide: Survival DC 14 (guide eases; past the edge, no guide helps).
- Hold direction in the deep wood: Survival DC 16 (DC 18 at the Still Heart).
- Endure the cumulative dread: Wis DC 14 → 16.
- Earn the presence's testimony: Insight/Persuasion DC 16 + reverence/offering.

## Starting Hook
The deep history (from Sennet's songs or the Hanging Oaks) points to the Old Mast itself; or the Gravecallers/Ledger are about to wake the presence and someone must reach the edge to stop it. Brann or Sennet brings the player to the boundary — and refuses to cross.

## Location
The Old Mast (`THE_OLD_MAST.md`) — the gated deep wood.

## Involved NPCs
Brann the Guide / Goodwife Sennet (to the edge only), the reckless Gravecallers (if the disaster-path is live), the deep presence (the gated climax), the Remnant survey-scholar (curious, kept at the edge).

## Involved Factions
Mourners'/woodfolk (keep it asleep), Gravecallers (would wake it), Concord Remnant (would study it).

## Surface Problem
Learn what sleeps in the deep wood — or stop it being woken.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The Old Mast holds a **pre-Concord landmark-power** the Concord routed its roads around; it stirs and resents the harvest's reawakening. It can grant an **oblique M5/M6 fragment** ("the dead were taken long before the roads fell, and are taken again") from outside the Concord. **STRICT CAP: a fragment and a warning — never the harvest mechanism, the Custodians, or the Hollow Court (which it does not know). The Root-Dark is off-limits/unbuilt. A low-level intrusion is signposted suicidal.**

## Objectives
1. Reach the Old Mast edge (guided). 2. Decide how far to go and why. 3. (If pursued) earn the presence's testimony, and/or stop it being woken.

## Possible Approaches
- **Reverent learning:** approach with offerings and woodcraft; earn the fragment and (maybe) the presence's favor.
- **Prevention:** stop the Gravecallers/Ledger from waking it (the disaster-prevention path; ties to TW-H15 and the Marked Oaks).
- **Restraint:** go only to the edge, take the warning, and turn back (a wise, valid choice).

## Obstacles
The misleading deep wood (getting lost is the chief danger); the cumulative dread; the watchers; the presence's test and price; the temptation (and the cult) to go too deep.

## Important Scenes
- The last offering-marks, and the guide turning back.
- The watchers warning the player back — and the choice to heed or press on.
- The Still Heart: the presence's regard, and the alien testimony (gated fragment).

## Clues
- The deep-wood truth (older than the Concord; the roads bend; gated shape).
- The oblique M5/M6 fragment (the presence's testimony; strictly capped).

## Combat Options
The watchers (awakened-tree/fey-bound-dead-like, AC 14, ~60 HP) — **they warn and turn back, fighting only the violent; retreat ends it.** The presence is **never a kill-target.** Force fails and gets the player lost. See the dungeon.

## Noncombat Options
Reverence, woodcraft, offering, and parley are the whole quest. Combat is a failure path.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Dread and the misleading wood are *designed to push the unworthy out alive*; following the dread back leads out; the watchers turn the player back non-lethally; the Root-Dark is off-limits; pressing past every warning strands/marks rather than instantly kills (at this tier). Heavily telegraphed throughout.

## Rest And Time Pressure
No safe rest in the Old Mast; Coldhearth is the haven days back. Pressure: if the disaster-path is live, the Gravecallers/Ledger are racing to wake the presence (the Old Mast clock near its end).

## Rewards
- The oblique M5/M6 fragment (the deepest Ring 1 corroboration of the harvest, from outside the Concord; gated/capped).
- The presence's favor (a one-time boon — true-rite blessing, safe wood-passage, or old-dead guidance) if earned reverently.
- The Old Mast kept asleep (the regional disaster averted) if the waking is stopped.

## Consequences
- **Reverent + asleep kept:** the player gains the fragment and the wood's favor; the region stabilizes.
- **Woken (failure/cult success):** a Tollwood-scale disaster — the deep wood spills its dead and dread toward the settlements and the East Road; a major regional consequence.
- **Restraint:** the player takes the warning and turns back; safe, with a partial fragment; entirely valid.

## Failure State
If the player can't reach the Still Heart, they keep the edge's dread and the partial truth, and leave alive (the wood ensures it). If they force it, they're turned back marked, not killed (at tier). The campaign is never blocked — the oblique fragment is a *bonus* corroboration, not a required gate (the main M5/M6 truth lives in the Reach and Caradril).

## Scaling Notes
- L6: the watchers turn the player back early; thin fragment; "you should not be here yet."
- L8: the Still Heart is reachable; the full oblique fragment and favor available.
- **Always gated:** never the Court, never the harvest mechanism, never the Root-Dark.
