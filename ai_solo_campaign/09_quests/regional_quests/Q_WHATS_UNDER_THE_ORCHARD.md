## AI Use
Develops AV-H16. The Vale's accessible Concord-ruin delve: the Buried Cloister, where Remnant scholars dig for the old Script. Delivers an M2/M6 *fragment* and M3 at levels 2–4, short of the great tomb, plus a rival-scholar dynamic and a tragic vigil-Remembrance.

## Quest Type
Regional / Mystery (M2/M6 fragment; M3)

## Level Range
2–4. Solo danger: low-moderate (unstable ruin; a sorrowful haunting).

## Likely Checks And DCs
- Strike a deal with / outmaneuver the Remnant diggers: Persuasion DC 13 / Stealth DC 13.
- Read the Scriptorium texts: Concord Script (reader required).
- Identify a Remembrance relic: Arcana/Religion DC 13 (or experiential).
- Settle the Vigil-Brother by rite: Religion DC 13.

## Starting Hook
The Remnant's Oneth Vael (or a curious local) speaks of a buried cloister under an Orchardmere orchard, full of old writing. The Remnant wants an escort/extra hands; or the player goes alone, ahead of the scholars.

## Location
The Buried Cloister (`THE_BURIED_CLOISTER.md`), south of Orchardmere.

## Involved NPCs
Sub-Lector Oneth Vael / his survey-pair (Remnant), the Vigil-Brother (Remembrance), Mother Combe (wants the Script destroyed or kept from the Remnant).

## Surface Problem
Explore and document the buried cloister.

## Hidden Truth (DM)
The cloister was a Concord wayside house that "settled the dead and kept their record for the shrine on the hill" — tying the Vale's ruins into a *network* and pointing at Saint Veddow's (M2/M6 fragment). Its relics store the dead (M3). **Act-paced: deliver the network-shape and the M3 demonstration, not the harvest's purpose pre-Act-3, never the Court.**

## Objectives
1. Get into the cloister (with, around, or ahead of the Remnant). 2. Read the Scriptorium. 3. Decide who gets the records, and free the Vigil-Brother.

## Possible Approaches
- **Cooperate:** work with the Remnant (opens the Script, advances their interest).
- **Compete:** beat them to the records (and choose what to do with them).
- **Reverent:** prioritize the Vigil-Brother and the dead over the loot.

## Obstacles
Unstable masonry; the flooded chapel; the sorrowful Vigil-Brother; the Remnant's competing claim.

## Important Scenes
- The Scriptorium Script naming "the shrine on the hill" (M2/M6 fragment; the network).
- A relic showing a dead face (M3).
- The Vigil-Brother's century-old, hopeless vigil — and his release.

## Clues
- The Scriptorium Script (C-AV-M2-1 / M6 fragment).
- A cloister Remembrance relic (M3).
- The Vigil-Brother's testimony ("they never came back for them" — M6 seed, capped).

## Combat Options
None required. The Vigil-Brother (Specter/ghost-like, AC 12, ~26 HP) defends only if looted disrespectfully; a scavenger beast is avoidable. See the dungeon.

## Noncombat Options
Negotiation, Script-reading, ritual; the whole quest.

## Solo-Play Safety Valves
Open retreat to the orchard; the Remnant camp as a fallback; the haunting is non-hostile to the respectful; ruin hazards are telegraphed.

## Rest And Time Pressure
Short rest safe in the ruin; long rest at the Remnant camp or Orchardmere. Soft pressure: dither, and the Remnant strips the Scriptorium and carries it south.

## Rewards
- M2/M6 fragment and M3 (act-capped clues).
- A Script-codex page (Remnant pays ~50 gp; Combe wants it kept from them).
- Mourners' goodwill if the Vigil-Brother is freed and the dead respected.

## Consequences
- **Player keeps/controls the records:** a clue and a choice (give to Combe, the Remnant, or hold).
- **Remnant gets them:** advances their reconstruction and their push for Saint Veddow's (Reclamation interest).
- **Ignored:** the Remnant strips the cloister; the Vigil-Brother keeps his vigil; the Ledger may later quarry it.

## Failure State
If the player can't read or recover the Script, they still grasp the *network-shape* (the cloister served "the shrine on the hill") and the thread reopens at Saint Veddow's. No dead end.

## Follow-Up Hooks
"The Sealed Door" (AV-H9; the hill the cloister served); "Saint's Bones" (M3); Combe's M6 path (gated); the Remnant's Reclamation thread (toward Caradril).

## Scaling Notes
- L2: a soft Script-and-haunting delve.
- L4: sharper Remnant rivalry; a second bound brother; a thin-touch relic.
