## AI Use

Locate quests by play-type so the AI DM can vary the texture of a session (don't run three dungeons in a row; mix investigation, social, travel, defense). Far-continent developed quests (Stage 10) are typed below; home-region quests carry their own types in `QUEST_INDEX.md`. Many quests support multiple approaches — the type is the *primary* mode.

---

## Investigation / Mystery (oblique)
*Solving a puzzle, tracing clues, reading a haunted/old site without necessarily fighting.*
- Q_VR_001 (the failing locks-node), Q_VR_004 (the bad water); Q_SALT_001 (the drowned town), Q_KM_001 (the gone-wrong mine); Q_MD_001 (the restless barrows); Q_SUN_002 (the far root-ache), Q_WS_003 (the thinning sky); Q_CH_002 (into the Crown), Q_CH_006 (the grave that won't keep). Plus home-region mysteries in `city_quests/` and `regional_quests/`.

## Dungeon / Ruin Delve
*Entering a discrete dangerous site with a guide, treasure, hazards, and a climax.*
- Q_SALT_002 (deep drowned town), Q_KM_001 (deep mine), Q_KM_002 (Old Iron fort), Q_HFH_004 (the Old Holds), Q_MD_002 (the deep barrow), Q_SUN_006 (the reclaimed ruin), Q_ET_003 (Cinderhold shaft), Q_CH_002 (the Crown). Home: `Q_BELOW_THE_STILLING.md`, `Q_THE_DROWNED_VAULT.md`, `Q_THE_HANGING_OAKS.md`, etc.

## Faction (alignment / patron / opposition)
*Working for, against, or between a faction; a faction-defining choice.*
- Q_VR_003 (Ledger/Crane), Q_VR_006 (Remnant); Q_GL_002/004; Q_HGP_007; Q_ET_004; Q_SP_001/007; Q_HFH_005; Q_SALT_003/004; Q_KM_002/004; Q_SUN_005/006; Q_MD_003/004; Q_CH_003 (Hollin Vane). Home: all `faction_quests/` chains.

## Politics / Diplomacy / Mediation
*Brokering, persuading, navigating councils, feuds, and power-blocs.*
- Q_VR_002 (the lord's debt); Q_GL_001/006; Q_HGP_001/005; Q_HFH_001 (the moot); Q_SALT_007 (the dying clan); Q_KM_003 (the strong hand); Q_WS_002 (the knotted kin); Q_SP_006 (rice-villages); Q_MD_003. Home: `Q_THE_OPEN_CHARTER.md`, `Q_THE_BASIN_VOTE.md`.

## Travel / Escort / Route-Opening
*Crossing dangerous country, escorting, opening a road between regions.*
- Q_VR_005 (the Eel); Q_SALT_005 (the white flats); Q_KM_007 (the pass out); Q_MD_005 (the road south); Q_WS_001 (the guest-law), Q_WS_005 (Spine-Foot); Q_SP_003 (smuggling); Q_HGP_002 (privateering); Q_CH_005 (the contested pass); Q_SUN_003 (lost pilgrims). Plus `fc_CROSS_REGION_TRAVEL.md`.

## Defense / Combat-forward (with solo safety valves)
*Holding a place or breaking a threat; always with allies, retreat, and noncombat off-ramps.*
- Q_KM_003 (warlord war), Q_KM_005 (the gate); Q_WS_004 (the Black Horse); Q_SUN_001/007 (grove-defense / the wilds answer); Q_CH_001 (defend Crownmouth). Home: combat-forward regional quests.

## Social / On-Ramp / Sanctuary
*Earning welcome, trust, or access; low-combat introductions to a region.*
- Q_SUN_004 (the green welcome); Q_WS_001 (the guest-law); Q_CH_001 (earning Crownmouth). Lighter social hooks throughout the `fc_*` files.

## Lore / Old-Songs / Network
*Carrying mystery-fragments, weaving the Mourner network, oblique revelation.*
- Q_SALT_004, Q_MD_004, Q_CH_006 (old-songs nodes); Q_VR_004, Q_HFH_002 (fresh failures); Q_SUN_002, Q_WS_003 (cosmic/hopeful far-echoes). Cross-region JOB_XR_001, H_XR_002.

## Anti-Cult / Moral Tragedy (Gravecaller mirror)
*Confronting the grief-cult's spread; a humane-vs-forbidden moral knot.*
- Q_SALT_006, Q_KM_006, Q_CH_004 (Father Ord). Home: `faction_quests/GRAVECALLERS/`.

## Low-Stakes / Local Color / Humane
*Small, character-driven, ignorable-but-rewarding texture.*
- Q_VR_007 (the free ferry); Q_SALT_007 (the dying clan, also feud); Q_SUN_004; many `fc_*` jobs (settle a single dead, ferry a refugee, mediate a dispute, crew a barge).

## Type Coverage Note (solo-play balance)
Per `QUEST_STANDARDS.md`: across all 82 far-continent developed quests, **every quest provides noncombat options and solo-safety valves**; no quest gates story progress behind a single roll or assumes a four-person party. Dungeon/defense/combat-forward types are the minority and all include allies, retreat, telegraphing, and alternate objectives.
