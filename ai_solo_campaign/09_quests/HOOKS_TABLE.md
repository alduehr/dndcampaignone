## AI Use

Ten session-sized Act 1 hooks, each tied to authored NPCs, factions, locations, and the mystery web. Surface them through the fiction (jobs, rumors, NPCs in need), never as a menu. Each lists its truth-link (DM-only) so the DM knows what it advances. **Hook 1 is the default opener.**

> Each hook gives recommended level, surface goal, hidden link, approaches (combat/social/stealth/investigation), a failure-still-progresses note, and a solo-safety valve.

---

### Hook 1 — "The One Who Came Back" (DEFAULT OPENER)
- **Level:** 1. **Source:** Sefra Quick offers it as an easy job; or the player simply arrives mid-crisis.
- **Surface goal:** The midwife Wren died and her rite failed — she's standing in her own doorway. The town wants someone to "deal with it" kindly. Talk to the Brevins; help.
- **Hidden link (DM):** M1/M5 — Wren is a Remembrance-revenant; the first clue toward the shrine. Sefra was paid to point a newcomer here (M8/M10 seed).
- **Approaches:** Social (speak with Wren/Tomas), ritual (fetch a Warden), folk (Wend), investigation (the grave-drift). Combat only if the town or player forces it.
- **Failure still progresses:** If Wren isn't peacefully resolved, a mob/Tomas-to-Gravecallers branch opens — new hooks, not a dead end.
- **Solo-safety:** Wren is not a default combat encounter; Hale/Othetha can assist; retreat is always possible.
- **Resolution → level 1→2.**

### Hook 2 — "Salt for the Sill"
- **Level:** 1–2. **Source:** Grandmother Wend / a frightened family.
- **Surface goal:** Rite-salt is running short as failures spike; fetch a fresh load from a Pale Coast trader (or recover a stolen shipment).
- **Hidden link (DM):** Builds Mourners' trust (toward Secret 15); the salt shortage is *because rites keep failing*, hinting M5.
- **Approaches:** Travel/social/investigation; possible smuggler negotiation or fen ambush (telegraphed).
- **Failure:** Town rites get worse; Circle anger rises (advances their clock).
- **Solo-safety:** Old Sashe can guide; the route is scoutable.

### Hook 3 — "A Quiet Job for the Ledger"
- **Level:** 1–2. **Source:** Factor Domic Sael.
- **Surface goal:** Recover a specific piece of Concord salvage from a nearby ruin and bring it to the counting-house. Good pay.
- **Hidden link (DM):** The "salvage" is a Remembrance relic (M3 seed); feeds the Ledger's monopoly (Secret 12/13). Domic's unease is a future lever.
- **Approaches:** Exploration/stealth/combat at a small ruin; or buy/trick it from someone else.
- **Failure:** The relic falls to a rival (Remnant/Gravecallers) — reroutes the clue, doesn't block it.
- **Solo-safety:** Small, scoutable ruin; retreat routes.

### Hook 4 — "The Councilor's Errand"
- **Level:** 2. **Source:** Councilor Reke (charming).
- **Surface goal:** Survey the basin shore "for the resettlement project" and report what ruins are surfacing.
- **Hidden link (DM):** Reke is steering the player toward the shrine (M2/M4/M8). What the player sees is real (surfacing shrine = M2 shape).
- **Approaches:** Exploration/investigation; meeting Old Sashe; reading Concord Script (gated).
- **Failure:** Reke shrugs and uses someone else; the basin clock advances anyway.
- **Solo-safety:** Daytime, shore-only; the deep shrine is telegraphed as too dangerous yet.

### Hook 5 — "The Reeve's Trust"
- **Level:** 2. **Source:** Reeve Coalmont (once the player seems honest).
- **Surface goal:** Quietly look into whether the basin scheme is really about water — Coalmont is uneasy but can't act publicly.
- **Hidden link (DM):** Direct path to M4/Secret 9 (Reke); allies the player with the honest Compact.
- **Approaches:** Investigation/social/surveillance; Pevin Oss's records; tailing Reke.
- **Failure:** Coalmont loses the council vote; the basin clock jumps a stage.
- **Solo-safety:** Social/legal; no forced combat.

### Hook 6 — "What the Fens Carry"
- **Level:** 2–3. **Source:** Old Sashe.
- **Surface goal:** A peat-cutter vanished in the Greyfens; help Sashe recover the body before the fog and the dead claim it.
- **Hidden link (DM):** M5 — Sashe shows the player the dead drifting toward the water (Secret 20); a direct shrine clue.
- **Approaches:** Exploration/survival/stealth; Remembrances (telegraphed, often avoidable).
- **Failure:** The body is lost to the shrine's pull — itself a chilling clue.
- **Solo-safety:** Sashe as guide; the fens are dangerous but navigable with respect.

### Hook 7 — "A Knock in the Night"
- **Level:** 2–3. **Source:** Tomas Brevin (secretly), or a grieving family.
- **Surface goal:** Someone is contacting the bereaved with promises of a "last word" — find out who (the Gravecallers) before the Compact purges them.
- **Hidden link (DM):** Door to the Gravecallers (M3/M6 truth-source); Tomas's secret (Secret 19); the Gravecaller Knock.
- **Approaches:** Social/investigation/stealth; the Tallow Man; Sister Knell.
- **Failure:** A purge happens; the Gravecallers radicalize (their clock advances).
- **Solo-safety:** The Gravecallers prefer to talk; combat is avoidable.

### Hook 8 — "The Scholar's Request"
- **Level:** 3. **Source:** Lector Briss (visiting from Caradril).
- **Surface goal:** Escort Briss to a Concord archive-cairn and protect him while he translates an inscription.
- **Hidden link (DM):** The player can begin **learning Concord Script** (the key clue gate); inscription seeds M6.
- **Approaches:** Travel/exploration/combat (cairn guardians/scavengers, telegraphed); social with Briss.
- **Failure:** The cairn is looted by Remnant agents or the Ledger — reroutes, doesn't block.
- **Solo-safety:** Briss is a (weak) caster ally; cairn is scoutable.

### Hook 9 — "The Failing Funeral"
- **Level:** 1–3 (scalable). **Source:** Any town; a public failed rite.
- **Surface goal:** A funeral fails publicly; the dead won't rest. Help the Warden (Hale) complete the rite or contain the situation.
- **Hidden link (DM):** Repeatable Act 1 texture; each failure nearer the basin is *worse* (M5); builds Warden trust toward Secret 8.
- **Approaches:** Ritual/social/combat (a single revenant, telegraphed)/investigation.
- **Failure:** A Remembrance escapes into the fens; a new minor threat seeds.
- **Solo-safety:** Hale assists; non-combat true-rite resolution available.

### Hook 10 — "Debts and the Drowned"
- **Level:** 3–4. **Source:** A family ruined by Ledger debt over a failed-rite tragedy.
- **Surface goal:** Help a family the Ledger is squeezing; the debt traces to the basin scheme's land grab.
- **Hidden link (DM):** Connects the Ledger's greed, the basin scheme, and the master plot (Secrets 9/12); a moral pivot on which faction the player favors.
- **Approaches:** Social/legal/investigation; confront Domic or Tallytooth Ren (telegraphed); expose paperwork.
- **Failure:** The family is ruined; the Ledger's grip tightens (its clock advances).
- **Solo-safety:** Primarily social/legal; enforcers threaten, rarely kill.
