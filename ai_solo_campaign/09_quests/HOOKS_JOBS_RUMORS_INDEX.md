## AI Use

Master index of all **short-form** hooks, rumors, and jobs/events (one-liners that point toward authored content), separate from full developed quests (`DEVELOPED_QUESTS_INDEX.md`) and major campaign questlines (`MAJOR_CAMPAIGN_QUESTS.md`). Surface these through fiction, never as a menu. ID prefixes: `H_` hook, `R_` rumor, `JOB_` job/event; region code in the middle (e.g. `H_GL_001`, `R_SALT_004`, `JOB_CH_002`). Truth status is in each table.
